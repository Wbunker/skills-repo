# Rates, Rate Plans, Bookings & Cancellation Policies

## Rate Plans in Hostaway → Booking.com

- Rate plans set in Hostaway push to Booking.com via API
- Each Hostaway rate plan must map to an **XML-enabled** Booking.com rate plan (not read-only)
- Minimum stay rules sync from Hostaway
- Cleaning fees and additional charges sync and are included in the commission calculation
- Dynamic pricing tools (PriceLabs, Wheelhouse, Beyond) integrated with Hostaway push automatically

### Rate Plan Markup

In Hostaway Listing Mapping → Settings, you can apply a channel-specific markup percentage for Booking.com. Default is 100% (no markup). Adjust to charge Booking.com guests more to offset commission.

## Booking Flow

- All Booking.com reservations are **instant** — there is no request-to-book option
- Reservation appears in Hostaway inbox in real-time
- Calendar blocks across all connected channels immediately
- Automated Hostaway message templates fire for Booking.com bookings
- Payment collected per your chosen model (Channel Collect VCC or Property Collect)

## Cancellation Policies

Configured per listing in Booking.com Extranet (or synced from Hostaway policies):

| Policy Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Fully Flexible** | Free cancellation up to a defined deadline (e.g., 24h, 48h, 1 week before check-in); full refund |
| **Semi-Flexible / Partial Refund** | Free cancellation before a deadline; percentage charge after |
| **Non-Refundable** | Guest charged full amount regardless of cancellation; often offered at discounted rate |
| **Custom** | Tiered — e.g., 100% refund at 30 days, 50% at 14 days, no refund within 7 days |

**Booking.com recommendation**: Allow free cancellation up to 1–2 days before check-in. Flexible policies rank higher and convert better.

**Custom seasonal policies**: Booking.com supports different policies for different date ranges (e.g., stricter during peak season).

### Prepayment Options

Hosts can require a prepayment (partial or full) at the time of booking, separate from the cancellation policy. Useful for securing revenue on longer stays or peak seasons.

### Host-Initiated Cancellations

Booking.com penalizes hosts for cancellations. Frequent host cancellations result in lower rankings and potential removal from search results. Avoid unless absolutely necessary.

## Minimum Stay & Availability

- Minimum stay rules sync from Hostaway
- **Note**: When you remap a listing in Hostaway, Booking.com resets minimum stay to empty — reconfigure immediately after any remapping
- Keep calendar availability open as far ahead as possible (12+ months) for maximum booking window
