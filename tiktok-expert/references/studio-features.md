# TikTok Studio — Creator Features

## What is TikTok Studio?

TikTok Studio is TikTok's unified creator hub (renamed from Creator Tools / Creator Portal). It is accessible at:
- **Web**: studio.tiktok.com
- **Mobile**: within the TikTok app (tap profile → hamburger menu → Creator tools → TikTok Studio)

TikTok Studio replaced the standalone Creator Center and consolidates video upload, analytics, monetization management, and editing tools.

---

## Video upload workflow

### Web upload (studio.tiktok.com) — recommended for scheduling and detailed settings

1. Click **Upload** — drag-and-drop or browse for file
2. Add caption (up to 4,000 characters)
3. Add hashtags (up to 5 — additional are ignored as of August 2025)
4. Tag location
5. Select cover/thumbnail: either choose a frame from the video timeline or upload a custom image (JPG/PNG)
6. Set interactions: toggle Duet, Stitch, Comments on/off
7. Set visibility: Public, Friends Only, or Private (Only Me)
8. Choose post time: **Post now** or **Schedule** (up to 10 days / ~240 hours in advance)
9. Click Post / Schedule

### In-app recording (mobile)

- Record up to **10 minutes** in-app
- Access effects, filters, text overlays, sounds, green screen
- Can trim, cut, merge clips
- Cover frame selection from video
- Duet/Stitch/Comments toggleable per video

### Desktop app

TikTok Studio desktop app provides a video editor with multi-track timeline, transitions, effects, text animations, and auto-captions. Targets creators wanting more powerful editing than the mobile app.

## Caption and description

| Limit | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Maximum characters | **4,000** (expanded from 2,200 in 2024/2025) |
| Recommended for readability | First 100–150 characters visible before "more" truncation |
| Emoji support | Yes |
| Hyperlinks | Not clickable; no links in captions |

Caption best practices:
- Place primary keywords in the first 150 characters (visible before truncation)
- Use keywords in three places: caption, on-screen text, and spoken voiceover
- Front-load the hook or topic statement
- TikTok now crawls captions for SEO — treat it like a search-optimized title

---

## Hashtags

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Maximum registered per post | **5** (limit implemented August 13, 2025) |
| Additional hashtags beyond 5 | Ignored by the algorithm |
| Recommended strategy | Mix: 1-2 broad, 1-2 niche/community, 1 trending (if relevant) |

Notes:
- Hashtag stuffing with irrelevant popular tags now **hurts** reach
- Algorithm prioritizes semantic relevance over tag popularity
- The 5-hashtag limit was introduced to reduce caption clutter and improve user experience

## Sound and music selection

- **TikTok sound library**: Royalty-free commercial sounds available for all creators
- **Trending sounds**: Using currently trending sounds can increase FYP distribution
- **Original audio**: Uploading with original audio creates a linkable sound that others can reuse on their own videos
- **Licensed music**: Available for personal accounts in most regions; business accounts have a more restricted commercial library
- Sound selection available during upload on web and mobile
- After posting, sound cannot be changed — must repost

## Duet, Stitch, and reactions — per-video settings

### Duet

A split-screen format where a user records alongside an original video. Both screens play simultaneously.

- Creator can enable or disable duet **per video** during upload settings, or retroactively via video privacy settings
- 2025 update: flexible layouts — side-by-side, top-bottom, green-screen (react mode), PiP
- "Duet chains" (duets of duets) now supported

### Stitch

A creator clips and appends up to **5 seconds** (updated to up to **10 seconds** in 2025) of another creator's video to the start of their own video.

- Toggleable per video by the original creator
- Can also be disabled globally in account Privacy Settings
- Original creator receives credit with a "stitched from @username" label

### Reactions

Video Reactions allow replying to a video with a video response (similar to Stitch but embedded alongside the original). Toggle available per video.

### Interaction defaults

When uploading, default toggles are:
- Comments: ON
- Duet: ON
- Stitch: ON

These can be changed per-video or globally via Settings → Privacy → Interactions.

## Cover / thumbnail selection

- **Frame selection**: Drag the slider to any moment in the video timeline
- **Custom thumbnail upload**: Upload a JPG or PNG image (recommended: 1080×1920 px)
- **Post-publish edit**: Cover can be changed within **7 days** of posting; availability may vary by region
- Custom thumbnails are valuable for Series and polished brand content

## Scheduled posting

| Feature | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Scheduling available on | Web (studio.tiktok.com) only — not in mobile app |
| Minimum lead time | 15 minutes |
| Maximum advance scheduling | ~10 days (240 hours) |
| Unified schedule calendar | Not available natively — must check each post individually |

Third-party tools (Later, Buffer, Hootsuite, Planoly, SocialBee) offer full scheduling calendars with TikTok publishing integration via the Content Posting API.

## Visibility settings

| Setting | Who can see |
|---------|-------------|
| Public (Everyone) | All TikTok users + non-logged-in viewers; eligible for FYP |
| Friends | Only mutual follows (people you follow who also follow you) |
| Private (Only Me) | Creator only — does not appear on FYP or profile |

Notes:
- Private accounts: "Public" still shows to followers; "Friends" shows to mutual follows only
- Visibility can be changed after posting via video settings
- Unaudited API clients can only post to Private (draft/inbox) mode

## Series

A Series is a collection of premium videos that viewers pay to unlock.

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Max videos per Series | 80 |
| Max video length per episode | 20 minutes |
| Price range | $0.99 – $189.99 USD |
| Creator revenue share | Not officially disclosed publicly; TikTok takes a platform fee |

Setup: Profile → Creator tools → Series → Create Series  
Viewers purchase access via in-app purchase; unlocked content appears in a dedicated tab on the creator's profile.

## TikTok Stories

- **Duration**: Up to 15 seconds per story; up to 60 seconds as a slideshow
- **Expiration**: 24 hours (standard Stories behavior)
- **Visibility**: Visible to followers via the Stories bar at top of Following feed and on creator's profile
- **Distribution**: Stories do **not** appear on the For You Page; reach is follower-limited
- **AI Alive**: TikTok's AI tool that converts static photos into dynamic Story videos (available in Story Camera)
- **Availability**: Rolling feature — not available to all accounts in all regions

Stories are primarily for follower-to-creator connection, not for new audience discovery.

## Other TikTok Studio features

### Auto-captions

TikTok Studio automatically generates closed captions (voice-to-text) for uploaded videos. Creators can edit for accuracy before posting. Auto-captions improve accessibility and also contribute to keyword indexing.

### Creator Search Insights

Shows what topics and keywords TikTok users are actively searching for. Accessible via TikTok Studio → Creator tools. Useful for content ideation and SEO.

### Q&A

A dedicated Q&A section on profiles where followers submit questions. Creators can respond with video answers that appear as content on their profile.

### LIVE Studio (streaming software integration)

For desktop streaming (OBS, Streamlabs, etc.), TikTok provides RTMP stream keys via TikTok Studio for PC-based LIVE broadcasts. Requires eligibility (1,000+ followers in most regions).
