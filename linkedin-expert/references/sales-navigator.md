## Lead Search Filters

Sales Navigator gives 50+ filters vs. LinkedIn's basic people search:

**Identity & Role**
- Job title (with Boolean operators)
- Seniority level (IC, Manager, Director, VP, C-suite, Owner)
- Job function (Marketing, Finance, Engineering, Sales, Operations, etc.)
- Current company / past company
- Geography (country, region, DMA-level)
- Industry

**Tenure & Recency**
- Years in current role
- Years at current company
- Changed jobs in last 90 days (Spotlight)

**Behavioral Signals**
- Recently posted on LinkedIn (last 30 days)
- Mentioned in the news
- Active on LinkedIn (engagement frequency)
- Buyer Intent signal (company showing purchase interest)

**Network**
- Connection degree (1st, 2nd, TeamLink connections)
- Shared experiences (school, company)

## Account Search Filters

16+ filters for finding target companies:

- Industry and sub-industry
- Company headcount (ranges)
- Headcount growth % (great for finding fast-growing companies)
- Annual revenue range
- Geographic headquarters
- Technology used (CRM, marketing platforms, specific tools)
- Funding stage (seed, Series A–D, public, etc.)
- Recent funding events
- Posted on LinkedIn recently

**Save accounts** to an Account List and get alerts on changes (new hires, executive departures, news mentions, headcount changes).

## Spotlights & Intent Signals

**Spotlights** surface warm outreach triggers — these are the highest-value filters in Sales Navigator:

| Spotlight | What it means |
|---|---|
| Changed jobs in last 90 days | New decision-maker, often has budget to spend and problems to solve |
| Posted on LinkedIn in last 30 days | Active on platform — message will be seen |
| Mentioned in the news | Great conversation opener (reference their news) |
| Followed your company | Already aware of and interested in your brand |

**Buyer Intent Signals (Core and above):**
LinkedIn tracks behavioral signals (profile views of your company, content engagement, competitor research) to flag accounts showing active purchase intent. These accounts should be prioritized for immediate outreach.

## AI Features (Advanced Plan)

**Account IQ:** AI-generated account summary including recent news, headcount trends, key relationships, strategic priorities. Replaces hours of manual research before an outreach call.

**Lead IQ:** AI-generated individual summary — career history, recent activity, shared connections, conversation starters.

**Message Assist:** AI-drafted InMail messages based on the lead's recent LinkedIn activity and your saved messaging context. Reduces drafting time significantly.

**Relationship Explorer:** Maps paths to a target decision-maker through shared connections across your network. Identifies warm introductions you may not have known existed.

**Smart Links:** Trackable content links you can share with prospects. Shows who clicked, how long they engaged, and which pages they viewed — insight you can use to time follow-ups.

**TeamLink:** Surfaces shared connections across your entire sales team (not just your personal network). Unlocks warm introduction paths through colleagues.

## Workflow Best Practices

1. **Build targeted Lead Lists** — don't work from raw search. Save leads to named lists (e.g., "SF Fintech VPs Q2") for organized, trackable outreach.

2. **Use Account Lists + alerts** — save your target accounts and monitor weekly alerts for trigger events (funding, new hires, news) before reaching out.

3. **Spotlights first** — filter for "Changed jobs" or "Recently posted" before broader outreach. These leads convert at higher rates.

4. **InMail > regular message for non-connections** — but remember InMail credits are refunded if the recipient responds within 90 days. Write thoughtful, personalized messages.

5. **Message Assist as a starting point** — use AI drafts as a starting point, then personalize with one specific observation about the lead. Generic AI-sounding InMails get ignored.

6. **Save searches** — set saved search alerts to be notified when new leads match your criteria. New matches are fresher opportunities.
