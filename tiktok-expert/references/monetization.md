# TikTok Monetization

## Overview of monetization programs

| Program | What it is | Key requirement |
|---------|-----------|----------------|
| Creator Rewards Program | Pay-per-view for long-form original videos | 10K followers, 100K views/30 days, 18+ |
| LIVE Gifts → Diamonds | Real-money gifts during LIVE streams | 1K followers, 18+, personal account |
| LIVE Subscriptions / Super Fan | Monthly subscriptions for exclusive content | LIVE eligibility + invitation by TikTok |
| Series (paid content) | Creators sell access to a collection of videos | Available to eligible creators |
| Subscription (non-LIVE) | Monthly fee for exclusive non-LIVE posts | Separate from Super Fan (splits Sept 2025) |
| TikTok Shop Affiliate | Commission on products promoted in videos/LIVEs | 1K followers (US), 18+ |
| TikTok Shop (own products) | Sell products directly via TikTok Shop | Merchant registration required |
| Creator Marketplace | Brand partnership matchmaking | 10K followers, 100K likes/28 days |
| TikTok Pulse | Premium ad revenue sharing on top-performing videos | Top 4% of creators (invite-only) |
| Tips | One-time fan tips during/after LIVE | LIVE eligibility |

## Creator Rewards Program

Launched **March 5, 2024** as the replacement for the Creator Fund ($0.02–$0.04 RPM). The Rewards Program pays significantly more and incentivizes longer, higher-quality content.

### Eligibility requirements

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Minimum followers | **10,000** |
| Views in last 30 days | **100,000+** |
| Minimum age | **18** (19 in South Korea) |
| Account type | Personal account only |
| Account location | Supported country (US, UK, Germany, France, Japan, South Korea, Brazil, Mexico, and others) |
| Account standing | No active violations; good standing |
| Video type | Original content only (not reposts/compilations) |

### How earnings work

Total reward = **Standard Reward** + **Additional Reward**

**Standard Reward**: Base payout calculated on qualified view count × RPM rate.

**Additional Reward**: Bonus tied to content quality metrics — watch time, retention, completion rate, and **Search Value** (how well content matches search queries).

### RPM rates (2025)

| Content type | RPM range |
|-------------|-----------|
| Standard creator content | $0.40 – $1.00 per 1,000 qualified views |
| High-retention niche content | Up to $6.00 per 1,000 qualified views |

Note: RPM varies significantly by niche, watch time, region, and Search Value score.

### Qualified views definition

A view is **qualified** when ALL of the following are true:
- Comes from the **For You Page** (FYP) — following feed views generally do not qualify
- **Lasts at least 5 seconds** for standard views; **30+ seconds** for Search-sourced views
- Video is at least **1 minute long**
- Video is original, not a repost or watermarked content from another platform
- View comes from a **supported country**
- No fraud or artificially inflated engagement

### Minimum video length
**1 minute** — videos shorter than 1 minute earn zero Creator Rewards regardless of views.

## Series (paid video collections)

Series allows creators to gate premium content behind a paywall.

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Max videos per Series | **80 videos** |
| Max length per video | **20 minutes** |
| Price range | **$0.99 – $189.99 USD** (creator sets price) |
| Content types | Pre-recorded videos only (not LIVE) |

Viewers purchase access to the full Series (not individual videos) via in-app purchase. Once purchased, the Series is accessible on the creator's profile under a dedicated tab.

Creators earn from each Series purchase minus TikTok's platform fee (not publicly specified but believed to follow standard app store revenue share norms).

## Subscription programs (2025 restructure)

TikTok announced a major split of its subscription product effective **September 15, 2025**:

### LIVE Subscriptions → Super Fan

All LIVE-related subscription perks moved to the new **Super Fan** program:
- Monthly fee set by the creator (**$2.99 – $99.99 USD/month**)
- Perks: sub-only LIVE access, exclusive LIVE stickers, sub badges, dedicated fan space
- Super Fan goals (community goal tracking)
- Creator receives **70% revenue share** + up to **20% bonus** = maximum **90%** (after app store fees)

### Non-LIVE Subscription

Separate subscription for exclusive non-LIVE content:
- Monthly fee set by creator (**$2.99 – $99.99 USD/month**)
- Exclusive subscriber-only posts (videos, photos, text)
- Custom perks, badges, stickers
- Same revenue split: 70% + up to 20% bonus = max 90%

## LIVE Gifts and Diamonds

See `live.md` for full details. Summary:

- Viewers buy Coins → spend Coins on virtual Gifts → creator receives Diamonds → cash out
- Base rate: 1 Diamond = $0.005 USD
- 2025 effective rate: 20%–53% of base (mission-based scaling)
- Minimum payout: ~$10 USD equivalent
- Payout via: PayPal, bank transfer (varies by region)

## TikTok Creator Marketplace

TikTok's official brand partnership platform connecting creators with brands for paid collaborations.

### Creator eligibility (2025)

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Minimum followers | **10,000** (50,000 in South Korea) |
| Video likes in last 28 days | **100,000+** |
| Recent posting activity | **3+ posts in last 28 days** |
| Minimum age | **18** |
| Account standing | Good standing, complete profile |

### How it works

- Brands search creator database using filters (niche, follower count, audience demographics)
- Brands post **campaigns** — creators can apply via **Open Application**
- Brands can send **direct invitations** to specific creators
- Collaboration types: video posts, LIVE integrations, Spark Ads boosts

### Spark Ads

Brands can use Creator Marketplace to boost creator content as **Spark Ads** — the creator's organic post becomes a paid ad without the creator re-recording. Creator must authorize the Spark Ads usage.

---

## TikTok Pulse

- Premium ad revenue sharing program
- Only available to the **top 4% of creators** by engagement (invite-only)
- Brands can place ads alongside top-performing content
- Creator receives **50% of ad revenue** from ads served against their videos
- Not widely available — considered an aspiration metric for large creators

---

## Tips

- Viewers can send one-time monetary tips to creators
- Available during or after LIVE sessions
- Not available in all regions
- Tips go directly to creator minus platform processing fee

## Monetization eligibility decision tree

```
Want to earn on TikTok?
├── Have 10K followers + 100K views/30 days + 18+?
│   └── YES → Apply for Creator Rewards Program (best per-view income)
├── Have 1K followers + 18+?
│   └── YES → Go LIVE → earn Gifts (Diamonds) + apply for Shop Affiliate
├── Have products to sell?
│   └── YES → Register as TikTok Shop merchant
├── Want to promote other brands' products for commission?
│   └── YES → Apply for TikTok Shop Affiliate (1K followers min)
├── Have 10K followers + 100K likes/28 days + 18+?
│   └── YES → Eligible for Creator Marketplace brand deals
└── Reached top 4% creator tier?
    └── YES → Apply for TikTok Pulse (invite-based)
```
