## Search Filters (Free)

Available on the Jobs tab search:

| Filter | Options |
|---|---|
| Location | City, state, remote, hybrid, on-site |
| Experience level | Internship, entry, associate, mid-senior, senior, director |
| Company | Specific companies |
| Company size | Employee count ranges |
| Job type | Full-time, part-time, contract, temporary, internship |
| Date posted | Past 24 hours, week, month |
| Industry | Any LinkedIn industry category |
| Easy Apply | Toggle to show only Easy Apply roles |
| "Under 10 Applicants" | Low-competition recent postings (highly valuable) |
| "Actively Hiring" | Companies with recent hiring activity |

**Boolean operators work in the keyword bar:** AND, OR, NOT, and quoted phrases.
Example: `"product manager" OR "program manager" NOT director`

## Job Alerts

- Set an alert per search query in the Jobs tab.
- Delivered via email or push notification.
- **Use daily frequency**, not weekly — competitive roles fill within 24–72 hours.
- Create multiple alerts for variations of your target title.

## Easy Apply vs. External Apply

| Factor | Easy Apply | External Apply |
|---|---|---|
| Effort | One click, uses LinkedIn profile | Full application on company site |
| Competition | Very high (often 10x more applicants) | Typically less competitive |
| Best use | When you have a warm connection at the company | Any role without a warm connection |

**Strategic approach:** Use Easy Apply only when you already know someone at the company or can get a referral — the internal advocate offsets the high applicant volume.

## Open to Work

Two modes:

**Public (green frame on photo):**
- Visible to all LinkedIn members
- 3x more recruiter messages
- May signal desperation to current employer and recruiters who prefer passive candidates

**Recruiters Only (invisible):**
- Only visible to LinkedIn Recruiter users
- 40% more recruiter InMails
- No visible banner — safe to use while employed
- Strongly recommended over the public banner when currently employed

*Settings:* Profile > Open to Work > "Recruiters only" option.

## Premium Career Advantages

Beyond free filters, Premium Career adds:

- **Applicant Insights** — see how you rank vs. other applicants by experience level and skills
- **Salary Insights** — salary range data for the specific role before applying
- **90-day profile viewer history** — see who viewed your profile (free shows 5 days)
- **InMail credits** — 5/month to contact hiring managers directly
- **"Under 10 Applicants"** filter — exclusive to Premium (also available free under some conditions)
- **"Companies Actively Hiring"** filter

Price: ~$29.99/month. Cancel after 6 months of active searching if not using it.

## Strategic Tips

**The "Under 10 Applicants" + Daily Alert combo:** Set a job alert with your target title/location, enable the "Under 10 Applicants" filter, set to daily. Apply within hours of the alert — early applications have significantly higher response rates.

**Use job postings as keyword sources:** Pull the exact skills and phrases from 5–10 target job descriptions. Add them to your profile's headline, About, and Skills sections to improve recruiter search visibility.

**Research the hiring manager:** Before applying, find the hiring manager or team members on LinkedIn. A personalized connection request with context (citing the role) sent before or simultaneously with applying can dramatically improve response rates.

**Company pages as research:** Visit the company's LinkedIn page before interviews. Check recent posts, employee headcount trends, news mentions, and recent hires. This intel is expected in senior-role interviews.
