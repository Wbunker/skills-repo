## Switching to Professional

1. Profile → Menu (☰) → Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account
2. Choose: **Creator** (influencers, artists, public figures) or **Business** (brands, retailers, services)
3. Select a category (e.g., Artist, Blogger, Restaurant, Clothing store)
4. Optionally connect a Facebook Page

**Creator vs Business — when to choose:**
- **Creator**: Content-first; want Subscriptions, Badges, Gifts; don't need full Shopping
- **Business**: Brand/retailer; need full Instagram Shopping, third-party CRM integrations, Business contact buttons, and Meta Ads ecosystem

You can switch between the two at any time without losing followers or content.

## Instagram Insights

Analytics dashboard for Professional accounts.

**Access:** Profile → Insights (or tap a post → View Insights)

### Account-Level Metrics
- **Reach**: Unique accounts that saw any of your content
- **Impressions**: Total times content was displayed
- **Profile visits**: How many people visited your profile
- **Followers**: Net gain/loss over the period; breakdown by day
- **Audience demographics**: Age, gender, top cities/countries
- **Content interactions**: Total likes, comments, saves, shares

**Time ranges:** Last 7 days, 30 days, 60 days, 90 days

### Post-Level Metrics
Per post (tap a post → View Insights):
- Reach, impressions, likes, comments, shares, saves
- Profile visits and follows from the post
- **Plays** (video/Reels): Total plays and average watch time
- **Accounts reached breakdown**: Followers vs. non-followers (shows how much content reached new people)

### Reels-Specific Metrics
- Plays, reach, likes, comments, shares, saves
- Average watch time and completion rate
- Accounts reached: followers vs. non-followers

### Stories Metrics
- Reach, impressions
- Interactions: replies, link clicks, sticker taps
- Navigation: forward taps, back taps, exits, next Story swipes

### Audience Best Times
Insights → Total Followers → Most Active Times — shows which days/hours your followers are most active. Use this to time your posts.

## Creator Monetization

### Subscriptions
Followers pay a recurring monthly fee for exclusive content.

**Requirements:**
- Professional Creator or Business account
- 10,000+ followers
- Must reside in an eligible country
- Comply with Partner Monetization Policies

**Price range:** $0.99–$99.99/month (creator sets the price)

**Subscriber-only content types:**
- Subscriber Stories (green ring, only subscribers see them)
- Subscriber Lives
- Subscriber posts (feed posts visible only to subscribers)
- Subscriber broadcast channels
- Subscriber close friends lists

**Managing subscriptions:** Professional Dashboard → Monetization → Subscriptions

### Live Badges
Viewers purchase badges during your Instagram Live to show support.

**Badge tiers:** $0.99 / $1.99 / $4.99
- Appear as hearts next to the viewer's username in Live chat
- Creators see total badges earned during and after the Live

**Requirements:** Eligible Professional account; 10,000+ followers (in most markets)

### Gifts
Viewers send virtual Gifts (purchased with Stars) on Reels.

- Stars are purchased by viewers; creators receive a share of the value
- Appear as reactions during Reel playback
- Track earnings in Professional Dashboard → Monetization → Gifts

### Reels Bonuses (Performance-Based)
Meta may invite eligible creators to earn bonuses based on Reels performance.

- By invitation only (not available to all accounts)
- Payout based on play count within a specific period
- Manage in: Professional Dashboard → Monetization → Bonuses

### Brand Partnerships & Branded Content
- Tag a brand partner on any post/Reel/Story using **Paid Partnership label** (required by FTC)
- Settings → Creator → Branded Content → enable "Require Approvals" for brand to pre-approve
- Brands can boost your branded content as an ad (with your permission)
- Use Creator Marketplace: instagram.com/creatormarketplace to find brand deals

### Affiliate Links
- Add affiliate product links directly to posts and Stories
- Commission earned when followers purchase through your link
- Manage in: Professional Dashboard → Monetization → Affiliate

## Instagram Shopping (Business)

Turn your Instagram profile into a storefront.

### Setup
1. Ensure you have a Business account (or Creator)
2. Connect to a Facebook Page
3. Create a product catalog in Meta Commerce Manager (commerce.facebook.com)
4. Submit for Shopping review in Instagram Settings → Business → Shopping
5. Once approved, tag products in posts, Reels, and Stories

### Shopping Features
| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Product Tags** | Tag products in feed posts, Reels, Stories |
| **Shop tab** | Dedicated shop tab on your profile |
| **Collection pages** | Group products into curated collections |
| **Checkout on Instagram** | In-app purchase (US only) — no website redirect |
| **Live Shopping** | Tag products during Instagram Live |
| **Product Launch** | Create countdowns for new product drops |
| **Shop Ads** | Promote individual products or collections |

### Catalog Management
- Sync catalog from Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, or other platforms
- Or manage manually in Meta Commerce Manager
- Product data: name, description, price, image, availability, URL

### Product Tagging in Content
- Feed posts: tap a person/product tag → select product from catalog
- Stories: product link sticker → search your catalog
- Reels: tag products before publishing
- All tagged products link to product detail page or checkout

## Content Strategy

### Posting Frequency (2026 benchmarks)
| Format | Frequency | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Feed posts | 3–5×/week | Photos and carousels |
| Reels | 3–5×/week | Highest reach format |
| Stories | 1–7 daily | Time-sensitive updates |
| Live | 1–2×/week | Community building |

### Content Mix
- 40% educational/value (tutorials, tips, how-tos)
- 30% entertainment (trending audio, relatable content, humor)
- 20% community/engagement (Q&As, polls, behind-the-scenes)
- 10% promotional (products, services, CTAs)

### Format Priority for Reach (2026)
1. **Reels** (reaches non-followers; highest algorithmic distribution)
2. **Stories** (daily touchpoint with existing followers)
3. **Carousels** (highest save rate; saves boost reach)
4. **Single images**

### Hashtags
- Use 3–10 highly relevant hashtags (quality > quantity since 2023 algorithm updates)
- Mix: 1–2 broad (1M+ posts), 3–5 mid-size (50K–500K), 2–3 niche (<50K)
- Add hashtags in caption or first comment
- Avoid banned hashtags (Instagram suppresses content using them)

### Timing
- Use Insights → Most Active Times for your specific audience
- General benchmarks: Tue–Fri 9am–12pm local time perform well, but audience-specific data trumps generalizations

### Engagement Signals That Boost Reach
1. Saves (strongest signal)
2. Shares (to DMs and Stories)
3. Comments (especially replies)
4. Likes
5. Profile visits from the post
