# TikTok Shop and E-Commerce

## Overview

TikTok Shop is TikTok's integrated e-commerce layer that allows products to be purchased directly within the app — from videos, LIVE streams, and a dedicated profile tab. Launched in the US in September 2023.

There are two distinct roles in TikTok Shop:

| Role | What they do |
|------|-------------|
| **Creator (Affiliate)** | Promote other sellers' products and earn commissions |
| **Merchant (Seller)** | List and sell their own products on TikTok Shop |

These roles are not mutually exclusive — a creator can also be a merchant.

---

## TikTok Shop for Creators (Affiliate Program)

### How it works

1. Creator browses the **TikTok Shop Affiliate Marketplace** for products to promote
2. Creator links products to their videos or LIVE streams
3. Viewer clicks the product link → purchases
4. Creator earns a **commission** (typically 5%–30% per sale, set by the seller)

### Eligibility requirements (US, 2025)

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Minimum followers | **1,000** |
| Minimum age | **18** |
| Account standing | Good standing, personal account |
| Country | US (other regions have different thresholds) |

Creators with fewer than 5,000 followers are initially enrolled in a **30-day Creator Pilot Program** before gaining full affiliate access.

### Collaboration types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Open Collaboration | Creator applies to promote any qualified shop's products |
| Targeted Collaboration | Seller specifically invites a creator to promote their product |
| Sample collaboration | Seller sends free samples; creator creates content |

### Product showcase in videos

- During video upload, tap **Link products** to attach products to your video
- A shopping cart icon appears on the video; viewers tap to browse/buy
- Works in both recorded videos and LIVE

### Commission rates

- Range: **5% – 30%** (varies by product category and seller)
- Category-specific base commissions are set by TikTok; sellers may offer additional incentives
- Commission paid after return window expires (typically 7–14 days after delivery)

## TikTok Shop for Merchants (Sellers)

### Setup

1. Register at **seller-us.tiktok.com** (US) or applicable regional Seller Center
2. Choose account type: Individual or Corporate
3. Provide required documents:
   - **Individual**: Government-issued ID + proof of address
   - **Corporate**: Business registration certificate
4. Link a TikTok account (required for Showcase tab integration)
5. Set up payment information (bank account)
6. Add products via Seller Center dashboard

### Seller Center

The merchant management portal (seller-us.tiktok.com) provides:
- Product listing management
- Order management and fulfillment
- Creator affiliate management
- Analytics (sales, conversion, traffic sources)
- Promotions and coupons
- Customer messaging

### Commission to TikTok

TikTok charges merchants approximately **8% commission** per sale plus a small transaction fee (structure may vary and is subject to change).

### Selling methods available to merchants

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Shoppable videos | Products tagged in regular feed videos |
| LIVE shopping | Products pinned and promoted during LIVE broadcasts |
| Showcase tab | Dedicated shopping tab on profile |
| Shop tab | TikTok's in-app marketplace discovery (products appear in browsing) |
| Spark Ads | Boost creator content that features your products as paid ads |

## Showcase tab

The Showcase tab is a dedicated storefront tab on a creator's or merchant's TikTok profile.

- Displays as a **shopping bag icon** on the profile
- Shows all approved products from linked TikTok Shop
- Viewers can browse and purchase without leaving TikTok
- Automatically populated once a TikTok account is linked to a TikTok Shop in Seller Center

### Creator showcase

Creators (affiliate partners) can also display their promoted products on a Showcase tab (when enabled). This allows profile visitors to browse products the creator recommends.

---

## LIVE shopping

One of TikTok Shop's highest-converting features. During a LIVE:
- Host pins products from their catalog to the screen
- Viewers see a shopping cart overlay; tap to view product details and buy
- Host can switch between pinned products during the session
- Real-time product comments and Q&A visible
- Commission or sale proceeds tracked automatically

---

## TikTok Shop affiliate product linking in video

**During upload (web)**:
1. After uploading video → look for "Add link" or "Tag products"
2. Search your Showcase products or linked affiliate products
3. Select product(s) to link
4. A shopping cart icon appears on the published video

**Maximum products per video**: Multiple products can be tagged.

## Key terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| Seller Center | Merchant backend portal for managing TikTok Shop |
| Showcase | Storefront tab on a creator/merchant TikTok profile |
| Open collaboration | Creator-initiated product promotion (no seller invite needed) |
| Targeted collaboration | Seller-initiated creator invitation for a product |
| Spark Ads | Promoted posts — organic content boosted as a paid ad |
| Commission rate | % of sale price paid to creator/affiliate per conversion |
| GMV (Gross Merchandise Value) | Total value of products sold — key metric in Seller Center |
