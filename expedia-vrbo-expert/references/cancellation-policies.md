# Cancellation Policies

**Location:** Dashboard > Rules and Policies > Cancellation Policy (Vrbo)  
**Or:** Property Details > Policies (Expedia Partner Central)

---

## Six Standard Policy Types

### Relaxed
| Window | Refund |
|--------|--------|
| 14+ days before check-in | 100% refund |
| 7–14 days before check-in | 50% refund |
| Under 7 days before check-in | No refund |

Best for: Hosts who want to attract budget-conscious guests and accept flexible changes.

---

### Moderate
| Window | Refund |
|--------|--------|
| 30+ days before check-in | 100% refund |
| 14–30 days before check-in | 50% refund |
| Under 14 days before check-in | No refund |

Best for: Most cabin operators — balances guest flexibility with host protection.

---

### Firm
| Window | Refund |
|--------|--------|
| 60+ days before check-in | 100% refund |
| 30–60 days before check-in | 50% refund |
| Under 30 days before check-in | No refund |

Best for: Peak season bookings or high-demand properties.

---

### Strict
| Window | Refund |
|--------|--------|
| 60+ days before check-in | 100% refund |
| Under 60 days before check-in | No refund (no partial) |

Best for: Holidays, long weekends, or properties with very limited inventory.

---

### Seasonal
Custom date-based policy — define different rules per season. Example:
- Summer (June–August): Firm policy
- Off-season (Nov–Mar): Relaxed policy

Best for: Campgrounds with dramatically different demand by season.

---

### Non-Refundable
| Window | Refund |
|--------|--------|
| Any time after booking | No refund |

Typically offered as a **discounted rate plan** alongside a refundable rate. Guests choose the lower price in exchange for no refund rights.

---

## Important Rules

- **Cancellation deadline:** 11:59 p.m. in the **property's time zone** on the specified day — not the guest's timezone
- **Refund basis:** Percentage applies to the **amount paid at the time of cancellation**, not the total reservation value
- **Pay-Per-Booking plan:** If the host retains payment on a canceled booking, Vrbo still charges the booking fee on the retained amount
- **Partial custom refunds:** Hosts can choose to refund more than the policy requires (goodwill gesture) but cannot refund less

---

## Deposit / Payment Policy

Separate from cancellation policy — controls when payment is collected:

- **Full payment at booking:** Guest pays 100% when they book
- **Deposit model:** Guest pays a partial amount at booking; balance due at a configured date before check-in

Configure payment timing in the payment/rates settings. The deposit amount and due dates are shown to guests during checkout and in confirmation emails.

---

## Damage / Security Deposit

Set a deposit amount displayed to guests in house rules and at checkout. This is separate from the payment deposit. Managed manually or through Vrbo's damage protection program (if enrolled).

---

## Refundable Rate Badge

Listings using **Relaxed** or **Moderate** policies qualify for a **"Fully Refundable" / "Free Cancellation"** badge in search results, which improves click-through rate and is prioritized in ranking. See [promotions.md](promotions.md) for details.
