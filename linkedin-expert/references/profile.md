## Profile Sections

**Photo** — Profiles with photos get 21x more views. High-res headshot, neutral or branded background.

**Banner/Cover Image** — Most users leave it default; customizing it is an easy differentiator. Communicate your value proposition.

**Headline (220 characters)** — Appears in every search result. Two proven formulas:
- *Recruiter-targeted:* `[Target Job Title] | [Key Skill] | [Value Proposition or Metric]`
- *Creator/B2B:* `I help [target audience] [achieve specific outcome]`
- Avoid vanity titles like "Visionary Leader" or "Guru."

**About Section** — Structure: professional story → who you help → proof of outcomes (metrics) → soft CTA (connect, message, visit site). First 2–3 lines appear before "See more" — the hook matters.

**Featured Section** — Pin posts, articles, links, or media. Use for proof: case studies, press mentions, lead magnets, project samples.

**Experience** — Each role: bullet-pointed outcomes with metrics, not just duties. Recruiter ATS scans this text.

**Skills** — Profiles with 5+ endorsed skills are 27x more likely to be found by recruiters. Match skills exactly to target job description language. LinkedIn Skill Assessments earn verified badges for technical skills.

**Recommendations** — Most trusted social proof signal. Give specific, outcome-focused recommendations first to encourage reciprocal ones. Request from managers and close colleagues.

**Education, Certifications, Licenses** — All searchable. LinkedIn Learning certificates auto-populate here.

## Keyword Strategy

1. Pull keywords from 5–10 target job descriptions.
2. Identify recurring skills, tools, certifications, and exact job title variants.
3. Place keywords in: headline, About section, Experience descriptions, and Skills section.
4. Update quarterly as industry terminology evolves.

## SSI Score (Social Selling Index)

A 0–100 score updated daily, based on last 90 days of activity. Four 25-point pillars:

| Pillar | What drives it |
|---|---|
| Establish Your Professional Brand | Profile completeness + publishing articles/content |
| Find the Right People | Using search and People You May Know |
| Engage with Insights | Commenting and sharing with added perspective |
| Build Relationships | Accepted connections, InMail replies received |

**Benchmarks:**
- Industry average: ~35
- Top 20%: 65+
- Thought leader tier: 75+
- 70+ score correlates with 2–3x higher organic reach and 40% better search ranking

**Key leverage:** Acceptance rate on connection requests matters more than volume. Targeted, personalized requests lift score; broad blasting hurts it.

View your SSI: `linkedin.com/sales/ssi`

## Creator Mode

Toggle in profile settings. When enabled:
- Primary profile CTA changes from "Connect" to "Follow"
- Unlocks newsletter publishing
- Shows your top 5 hashtags on profile
- Provides creator analytics (follower growth, post reach, demographics)

**Best for:** Anyone posting content regularly, building an audience, or monetizing thought leadership. Does not prevent people from connecting — just changes the default button.

## Completion Checklist

- [ ] Professional photo
- [ ] Custom banner
- [ ] Keyword-rich headline (all 220 characters used)
- [ ] About section with hook in first 2–3 lines and soft CTA
- [ ] Current experience with metric-driven descriptions
- [ ] 5+ endorsed skills matching target job descriptions
- [ ] 2–3 specific, outcome-focused recommendations
- [ ] Education complete
- [ ] Location and industry set
- [ ] Featured section with at least one proof piece

**Open to Work options:**
- *Public (green banner):* Visible to all members — 3x more recruiter messages but may signal desperation to employers.
- *Recruiters only:* Invisible to regular members; visible only to LinkedIn Recruiter users — 40% more recruiter InMails. Strongly preferred when currently employed.
