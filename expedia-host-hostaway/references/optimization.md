# Expedia Listing Optimization & Best Practices

## Content Score (0–100)

Content Score is Expedia's measure of listing quality and directly determines search placement. Access it in EPC under your property's performance dashboard.

### Score Components
1. **Property Amenities** — completeness of shared/building amenities
2. **Room Amenities** — completeness of in-unit features
3. **Policies & Deposits** — cancellation policy, deposit terms defined
4. **Photos** — quantity, resolution, coverage

### Target Score
Aim for 80+. Top-ranked listings consistently have near-perfect scores. The top listings in search results always get the most clicks — score is the primary lever you control.

---

## Photo Optimization

Photos are the single highest-impact content improvement:

- Upload **20–30 photos** for most vacation rentals
- Minimum resolution: 2,880px wide
- Cover every space: bedroom(s), bathrooms, kitchen, living area, outdoor space
- 1 photo per major amenity (pool, hot tub, game room, fire pit)
- 1 exterior shot, 1 entrance/approach shot
- Properties with unique room-type photos average **11% higher conversion**
- Use professional photography — Expedia's audience has hotel-level expectations

---

## Amenities — List Everything

Travelers filter by amenities. Missing = invisible in filtered search.

Priority amenities to list (if applicable):
- Kitchen / full kitchen
- Washer/dryer
- Free parking
- Pool / hot tub
- Pet-friendly
- Air conditioning / heating
- Wi-Fi
- EV charging
- Accessibility features

## Pricing & Visibility

### Competitive Pricing
"Offer Strength" (one of two main ranking signals) is based on rate competitiveness. Use Hostaway's dynamic pricing integrations (PriceLabs, Wheelhouse, Beyond) — they sync to Expedia automatically.

### Accelerator (EPC Tool)
Temporarily increase your commission to Expedia to boost search placement during low-demand periods. Useful for:
- Filling last-minute gaps
- Building review velocity on a new listing
- Soft seasons

### Rev+ (EPC Tool)
Expedia's market benchmarking tool. Shows competitor rates and demand data. Use to validate your pricing strategy.

---

## Cancellation Policy Strategy

Expedia's own data shows **flexible cancellation policies improve search visibility** and booking conversion. Consider:
- Free cancellation up to 7–14 days before check-in for standard bookings
- Non-refundable option with discounted rate as a secondary rate plan

Stricter policies reduce conversion and hurt your Offer Strength score.

---

## Guest Experience Score

The second major ranking signal. Impacted by:
- Review scores (star ratings)
- Refund rate (too many refunds = flag)
- Relocation rate (moved guests = serious penalty)
- Complaint volume

**Protect your score:**
- Accurate listings prevent expectation mismatches
- Respond to issues promptly before they escalate to Expedia support
- Avoid situations that force Expedia to intervene (leads to frozen reservations and score damage)

---

## Review Strategy

- Expedia sends review requests to guests automatically post-stay
- You cannot solicit reviews off-platform
- Respond professionally to all reviews (public responses visible to future guests)
- High review scores compound: better rankings → more bookings → more reviews
