# Booking.com Listing Content

## Content Syncs FROM Hostaway

With the API connection, listing content pushes from Hostaway to Booking.com:
- Property description
- Amenities (all)
- Booking policies
- Room details and capacity
- Photos
- Taxes and fees

Update content in Hostaway; it syncs to Booking.com. You can still edit directly in the Extranet, but Hostaway may overwrite changes on the next sync.

---

## Content Score

Booking.com's **Content Score** (0–100%) measures listing completeness and quality, and directly affects search visibility. Check it in the Extranet under the Property section → Property page score.

Key components:
1. **Photos** — quantity, quality, coverage
2. **Property description** — completeness and length
3. **Amenities** — how many are listed and verified
4. **Policies** — cancellation, house rules, check-in/out defined

Target 90%+ for strong visibility.

## Photo Standards

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| Minimum | 5 photos (10+ strongly recommended) |
| Recommended resolution | 4,000 × 3,000 px |
| Minimum resolution | 2,048 × 1,080 px |
| 360° photos | 2:1 aspect ratio, min 1,280 × 900 px |
| Cover shots | Booking.com controls which image shows in search — you cannot choose |

Cover all rooms: bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchen, living area, outdoor/amenity spaces.

Professional photography recommended — Booking.com's guest audience has hotel-level expectations.

---

## Amenities

List every available amenity — guests filter search by amenities, so missing items mean missed appearances.

Priority for vacation rental guests:
- Full kitchen / kitchenette
- Washing machine / dryer
- Free parking
- WiFi
- Air conditioning / heating
- Pool / hot tub
- Pet-friendly
- Workspace / desk
- Balcony / terrace
- EV charging

---

## Property Description

- Booking.com **auto-generates** an initial description from your property details — review and customize it
- Specify property type and layout clearly (bedrooms, bathrooms, sleeping arrangements)
- Highlight unique selling points, views, location advantages
- Include nearby attractions and transport
- Indicate whether self-catered (important for vacation rentals vs. hotel rooms)
- Keep tone professional — Booking.com skews toward business and international travelers

---

## House Rules & Policies (Set in Hostaway)

Define clearly:
- Check-in / check-out times and self-check-in instructions
- Minimum guest age
- Pet policy (types, size limits, fees)
- Events / parties permitted or not
- Smoking policy
- Quiet hours
- Additional guest fees

Guests see these at booking — gaps lead to disputes and negative reviews.

---

## Property Types Accepted

Booking.com accepts: apartments, condos, single-family homes, townhouses, villas, cottages, and most vacation rental property types.
