# Promotions & Visibility

## Accelerator (Pay-Per-Stay Visibility Boost)

**Location:** Marketing tab > Accelerator

The primary tool for boosting search ranking during slow periods or for specific date ranges.

**How it works:**
1. Select target dates
2. Choose a compensation level (higher = more boost)
3. Preview the estimated sort order impact
4. Activate — no upfront cost

**Cost model:** Pay-per-stay — charged only when a guest **completes a stay**, not per click or booking. No financial risk if the stay doesn't happen.

**Results:** Properties using Accelerator see ~20% higher gross booking value and ~20% more net room nights on average.

**Best use cases:**
- Slow shoulder-season dates
- Specific weekday gaps
- Newly listed cabins building review history

## Members Only Rates

Target Expedia Group's **One Key** loyalty program members (Blue, Silver, Gold tiers).

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Conversion vs. standard | 5x higher |
| Average discount | 15% |
| Discount for max benefit | 20%+ |
| Booking value uplift at 20%+ | Up to 25% higher |

Members book 2.5x more frequently than non-members. Available at 320,000+ properties.

**Setup:** Create a Member-Only rate plan in the Rates & Availability section.

## Refundable Rate Labels

Listings with flexible cancellation policies automatically receive **"Fully Refundable"** or **"Free Cancellation"** badges in search results. These labels:
- Are prioritized in search ranking
- Display a visual trust badge
- Require no additional setup — configured via cancellation policy settings

See [cancellation-policies.md](cancellation-policies.md) for policy options.

## Package Deals

Bundle cabin stays with flights and/or car rentals through the Expedia Group marketplace. Configured as a Package rate plan.

**Benefits:**
- 32% of travelers prefer bundled discounts
- Typically results in longer stays and higher daily rates

Additional rate plan configurations:

## TravelAds (Sponsored Listings)

**Model:** Pay-per-click advertising

**Reach:** 200+ Expedia Group websites

**How to access:** Separate TravelAds platform (not within Partner Central directly) — create an account at the TravelAds platform.

**Controls:**
- Customizable daily budget
- Bidding strategy options
- Audience and destination targeting

TravelAds complement Accelerator — Accelerator boosts organic ranking (pay-per-stay), TravelAds buys paid placement (pay-per-click).
