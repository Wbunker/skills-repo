# VRBO Listing Content

## Content Syncs FROM Hostaway (Unlike Expedia)

With a VRBO API connection, listing content is **exported from Hostaway to VRBO**. This is the opposite of Expedia, where content must be managed in Expedia Partner Central.

What syncs from Hostaway → VRBO:
- Property name and description
- Amenities (room-level and property-level)
- Photos
- Maximum guest capacity
- Taxes and fees
- Rates and availability

Update content in Hostaway; it will push to VRBO. You can still update directly in VRBO if needed, but changes may be overwritten by the next Hostaway sync.

---

## Required Listing Fields

- Property address (verified)
- Accurate location, size, and sleeping arrangements
- At least 6 photos (20+ recommended)
- All available amenities listed
- Cancellation policy selected
- Pricing and minimum stay set
- Host identity verified (government-issued ID + valid payment method — 2025 requirement)

---

## Photo Standards

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| Minimum | 6 photos |
| Recommended | 20+ |
| Optimal resolution | 3,840 × 2,160 px (4K) or higher |
| Minimum resolution | High quality; no blurry or stock images |
| Orientation | Wide-angle preferred to show space |
| Lighting | Natural daylight; avoid harsh flash |

Cover: all bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchen, living area, outdoor spaces, and any featured amenity (pool, hot tub, game room).

Stock images are not allowed. Professional photography strongly recommended.

---

## Amenities: List Everything

VRBO guests filter search results by amenities. Missing amenities = invisible in filtered searches.

High-priority amenities to list:
- Kitchen / full kitchen
- Washer/dryer
- Free parking / EV charging
- Pool / hot tub
- Pet-friendly
- Air conditioning / heating
- Wi-Fi
- Waterfront / view
- Accessibility features

VRBO's **sustainable stay badge** (2025) appears on listings with 3+ verified eco-friendly features — a visibility booster.

---

## Property Description

- Lead with the most compelling features (views, pool, location)
- Include neighborhood context and proximity to attractions
- Accurately describe sleeping arrangements (beds, pullouts, bunk beds)
- List house rules clearly (check-in/out times, noise, pets, events)
- Incorporate relevant keywords for search (beach house, mountain cabin, etc.)
- Do not exaggerate — mismatches between listing and reality drive negative reviews

---

## Identity Verification (2025 Requirement)

VRBO now requires all hosts to complete identity verification:
- Government-issued photo ID
- Valid payment method on file

This is a VRBO account-level requirement, not per-listing. Complete in VRBO owner dashboard.
