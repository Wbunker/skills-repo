# Booking.com Payments for Hosts

## Two Payment Models

Choose per property in the Booking.com Extranet. This choice determines how and when you receive money from bookings.

---

## Channel Collect (Payments by Booking.com)

Booking.com collects payment from the guest online. The host receives a **Virtual Credit Card (VCC)** per booking.

**Flow:**
1. Guest pays Booking.com at booking or check-in
2. Booking.com issues a unique VCC for that reservation
3. Host charges the VCC (like a regular Mastercard) through their payment terminal or Hostaway

**VCC Details:**
- Each booking gets a unique card number, expiration date, and CVC
- Activation date is set by Booking.com (often around check-in or after stay)
- VCC is visible in Hostaway under the reservation's payment details
- **Must be charged within 12 months of checkout** — funds are forfeited after this window
- Check VCC status in Hostaway: look for "Paid online" under the reservation

**Hostaway Auto-Payments for VCC:**
- Configure Hostaway to automatically charge VCCs upon activation
- Go to: Hostaway → Channel Manager → Booking.com → Payment settings
- Reduces manual work and risk of forgetting to charge a VCC

---

## Property Collect (Hotel Collect)

The host collects payment directly from the guest — by card at check-in, cash, or pre-authorized payment. Booking.com only tracks the commission owed.

**When to use:** When you want full control over payment timing and method, or you prefer direct guest payment.

**Important:** You are responsible for collecting payment. Booking.com does not guarantee payment if a guest no-shows or disputes in Property Collect mode.

---

## Monthly Commission Billing

Booking.com does **not** invoice per booking. Instead:

- Invoice issued **3rd–6th of each month** covering all reservations with checkout dates in the prior month
- **Review window**: Until the 2nd or 5th, hosts can adjust no-shows, fix pricing errors, or update dates before the invoice finalizes
- Commission applied to: nightly rate + cleaning fees + additional charges (excluding taxes)
- Pay the invoice via Booking.com's payment portal

**Standard commission: 10–25%, typically ~15%** for vacation rentals.

---

## No-Shows

- If a guest no-shows and your policy allows retention of payment, you owe commission on the retained amount
- Mark no-shows in the Extranet before the invoice review window closes to avoid incorrect commission charges
- For Channel Collect, the VCC may have limited funds if the guest's payment failed — check before charging

---

## Refunds

- Refunds for cancellations within policy: issued back to guest's original payment method
- No commission owed on fully refunded bookings
- Partial refunds: commission applies to the amount retained
