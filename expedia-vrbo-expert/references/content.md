# Property Content

## Photos

### Requirements & Recommendations

| Spec | Minimum | Recommended |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Resolution | 1,000 px | 2,880 px+ |
| Quantity | 6 (for Active status) | 10–30 total |
| Per unit type | — | 4 photos |
| Orientation | — | Horizontal preferred |

### What to Photograph
- Exterior of each cabin
- Living areas, bedrooms, kitchen
- Bathrooms (61% of travelers rate these as very important)
- Unique amenities (fireplace, hot tub, deck)
- Campground amenities (rec area, pool, common spaces)

### Photo Score
The **Photo Score** in Partner Central highlights missing photo categories and areas for improvement. Check it via the listing's Property Details section.

### Impact
- Properties with unique photos per room type average up to **11% higher conversion**
- Adding 4–5 unique photos per unit type can yield significant ADR increases

### How to Upload
- Browser upload in Property Details
- Mobile app camera (includes built-in Photo Score feedback)

## Descriptions

**Location:** Property Details section

**Minimum:** 400 characters (required for Active status)

**What to include:**
- Cabin-specific amenities and features
- Campground amenities and services
- Policies summary (pets, smoking, etc.)
- Proximity to local attractions, trails, water access
- Check-in/check-out procedures

---

## Amenities

Amenities are split into two categories — both matter for search filter inclusion.

### Room Amenities (In-Cabin)
Examples: TV, fireplace, full kitchen, dishwasher, air conditioning, hot tub, Wi-Fi, private deck

### Property Amenities (Campground-Wide)
Examples: swimming pool, recreation area, laundry, camp store, playground, pet area, fire pits

**Critical:** If an amenity is not listed, the cabin is excluded from search results when guests filter by that amenity. Guests who need a specific amenity (e.g., pet-friendly, Wi-Fi) will never see the listing.

## Points of Interest

Add nearby attractions with optional photos:
- Hiking trails, state parks
- Restaurants, local dining
- Shops, outdoor outfitters
- Water access, fishing spots

Nearly 90% of guests want location context when choosing a rental.

## Policies

**Location (Vrbo):** Rules and Policies section  
**Location (EPC):** Property Setup or Property Details section

Policies to configure:
- Check-in and check-out times
- Minimum age to book
- Pet policy (allowed/not allowed, fees)
- Smoking policy
- Event/party policy
- Maximum occupancy per cabin
- House rules (noise, parking, etc.)
