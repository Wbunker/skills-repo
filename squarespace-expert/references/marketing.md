## Email Campaigns

**Access:** Admin → Marketing → Email Campaigns

**Setup:**
1. Create sender profile (name, email address, physical address — required by CAN-SPAM)
2. Build mailing list (import CSV or collect via forms/checkout opt-in)
3. Create campaign from template or blank

**Campaign creation:**
- Drag-and-drop email builder
- Branded templates with your site's colors and fonts
- Import content from existing blog posts, products, or logo
- AI-generated email copy from brand prompts
- Mobile preview

**Sending:**
- Send immediately or schedule for later
- A/B testing not available natively (use integrations)
- Unsubscribes handled automatically

**Integrations:** Mailchimp (sync mailing list), Zapier

## Social Media

**Connected platforms:** Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, X (Twitter), LinkedIn, Foursquare

**Selling via social:**
- Instagram Shopping: sync product catalog, tag products in posts
- Facebook Shop: sync product catalog via Facebook Commerce Manager
- Pinterest Catalog: sync products to Pinterest shopping

**Content creation:**
- Unfurl app: social content creation with templates, filters, fonts, stickers

**Blog → Social:** Auto-share new blog posts to connected accounts

## Analytics

**Access:** Admin → Analytics (also available in mobile app)

**Traffic metrics:**
- Pageviews and unique visitors
- Traffic by source: search, social, direct, referral
- Top content pages
- Visitor geography
- Traffic spikes and trends over time

**Commerce metrics:**
- Revenue and orders over time
- Top-selling products
- Units sold per variant
- Conversion rate (add to cart → purchase)
- Abandoned cart data

**Email metrics:**
- Open rate, click rate, unsubscribes per campaign

**Google Analytics integration:** Paste GA4 Measurement ID in Admin → Settings → Advanced → External Services

## SEO Tools

**Built-in (Admin → Marketing → SEO):**
- SEO checklist with status indicators
- Connect Google Search Console
- Sitemap URL for submission
- Site-level title format (e.g., "Page Title — Site Name")
- Page-level SEO titles and descriptions
- Google Business integration guidance

**Indexing controls:**
- Hide from search engines per page (advanced settings)
- Noindex entire site during development

**Structured data:** Automatic JSON-LD for products, events, recipes, articles

## Promotions & Engagement

**Promotional Pop-Up (Admin → Marketing → Promotional Pop-Up):**
- Trigger: time delay, exit intent, or scroll depth
- Content: text, image, or discount code reveal
- Display rules: all pages, specific pages, frequency cap

**Announcement Bar (Admin → Marketing → Announcement Bar):**
- Persistent banner across top of site
- Dismissable or permanent
- Link to page or external URL

**Contacts Tool:**
- View all contacts (customers, subscribers, form submitters)
- Segment by: has account, is customer, accepts marketing
- Email individual contacts directly

## Acuity Scheduling

**What it is:** Appointment booking system integrated into Squarespace (separate product, included in some plans or as add-on)

**Access:** Admin → Scheduling or separate Acuity Scheduling login

**Setup:**
1. Set availability (hours, blocked times, buffer times)
2. Create appointment types (duration, price, intake form)
3. Embed booking page on Squarespace site
4. Configure confirmations and reminders

**Appointment types:**
- One-on-one appointments
- Group classes with capacity limits
- Packages (bundled sessions)
- Subscriptions (recurring access)

**Calendars:**
- Starter plan: up to 6 calendars
- Professional plan: up to 36 calendars

**Client experience:**
- Self-scheduling via booking page
- Automatic time zone detection and conversion
- Custom intake forms before session
- Reschedule/cancel via confirmation link

**Payments:**
- Collect payment at booking: Stripe, Square, PayPal
- Deposits and tips
- Invoices, bundled packages, memberships, gift certificates

**Notifications (automated):**
- Confirmation emails/SMS
- Reminder emails/SMS (configurable timing)
- Cancellation and rescheduling notifications
- Follow-up emails post-appointment

**Video conferencing:** Auto-generate links for Google Meet, Zoom, or GoToMeeting per booking

**Custom API:** Available on Professional plan for advanced integrations

## Invoicing

**Access:** Admin → Invoicing (or Acuity Scheduling)

**Features:**
- Create one-time invoices with itemized line items
- Recurring invoices (scheduled automated delivery)
- Proposals, estimates, and contracts
- Client management: invoice history, notes
- Online payment collection via link

**Client portal:** Clients can view and pay invoices online
