# VRBO Ranking & Optimization

## How VRBO Search Ranking Works

VRBO's algorithm prioritizes listings based on:

1. **Instant Book enabled** — strongest single ranking factor; instant-book listings rank higher than request-to-book
2. **Calendar freshness** — calendar must be updated at least every **60 days** or VRBO may hide the listing from search
3. **Review score** — higher scores rank higher; 4.4+ required for Premier Host
4. **Response time** — reply within 1 hour; never exceed 24 hours
5. **Acceptance rate** — declining requests hurts ranking; 95%+ required for Premier Host
6. **Premier Host status** — badge + Boost access gives systematic ranking advantage
7. **Listing completeness** — verified amenities, clear house rules, correct calendar sync
8. **Competitive pricing** — rates benchmarked against comparable listings in your market

---

## Instant Book: Enable It

Instant Book is the highest-leverage ranking change available. Benefits:
- Higher search placement
- No risk of missing the 24-hour acceptance window
- Guests prefer instant confirmation; higher conversion rate

**Set it up**: VRBO owner dashboard → listing settings → booking type → Instant Book.

If screening guests is a concern, use VRBO's Instant Book with conditions (verified ID, positive reviews) rather than Request-to-Book.

---

## Calendar Hygiene

- **Update your calendar at least every 60 days** — even if nothing changes. Log in and make a small adjustment (block/unblock a date temporarily) if needed just to trigger a refresh.
- Keep availability at least 12 months out for maximum booking window
- Daily calendar checks recommended
- Ensure no stale iCal links are creating phantom blocks

---

## Photo Optimization

Photos are the top driver of guest click-through:
- 20+ photos (minimum 6 required)
- 4K resolution preferred (3,840 × 2,160)
- Cover all rooms, outdoor spaces, and amenities
- Natural light, wide-angle shots
- Professional photography pays for itself quickly
- Sustainable/eco features? Add the **sustainable stay badge** (3+ verified eco features)

---

## Pricing Strategy

- Use dynamic pricing (PriceLabs, Wheelhouse, Beyond) — syncs to VRBO via Hostaway
- Competitive opening rates for new listings build review velocity faster
- Offer **flexible cancellation** (Moderate policy) — converts more bookings
- Weekend vs. weekday pricing differentiation helps yield management
- Seasonal minimum stays (higher in peak, lower in shoulder) optimize occupancy

---

## Review Strategy

VRBO sends review requests to guests automatically post-stay. Host actions:
- Use Hostaway automated post-stay messages to remind guests to review
- Respond professionally to every review (public response visible to future guests)
- Address negative reviews calmly and constructively — shows future guests you're attentive
- Do not incentivize reviews off-platform (violates VRBO policy)

High review volume + high score compounds: better ranking → more bookings → more reviews.
