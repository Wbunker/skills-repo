# Booking.com Visibility Programs

## Overview

Booking.com offers two opt-in programs that trade higher cost for greater visibility. Both can be used simultaneously and target different levers (discounts vs. commission).

| Program | Cost to Host | Visibility Boost | Eligibility |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Genius** | 10–20% discount (funded by host) | +70% search views, +45% bookings | Open to all; automatic for eligible properties |
| **Preferred Partner** | +3% commission (~18% total) | +65% page views, +40% bookings | Top 30% of partners, 70%+ performance score, 7.0+ review |
| **Preferred Plus** | +8% commission (~23% total) | +60% visibility above standard Preferred | Top 10% of Preferred Partners |

---

## Genius Program

Booking.com's guest loyalty program has three tiers (Genius Level 1, 2, 3). Properties participating in Genius offer discounts to loyalty members.

**How it works:**
- You set a discount (10–20% minimum) for Genius guests
- Booking.com surfaces your listing more prominently to its large Genius traveler base
- **No extra commission** — the discount comes directly out of your payout
- Enrollment is through the Booking.com Extranet → Opportunities tab

**Who it's for:** Properties wanting more bookings and willing to reduce margins for volume, especially in competitive markets or for filling shoulder seasons.

**Who to avoid it:** Properties running near full occupancy — the discount erodes margin without filling incremental nights.

---

## Preferred Partner Program

Booking.com elevates listings with the Preferred badge in search results.

**How it works:**
- Booking.com adds ~3% to your standard commission rate (total ~18%)
- No action required by host — Booking.com invites eligible properties
- Must maintain: top 30% of all partners, 70%+ performance score, 7.0+ review score
- Properties that slip below thresholds lose Preferred status

**Who it's for:** High-performing properties wanting maximum visibility and willing to pay for it via higher commission.

---

## Preferred Plus

Higher-tier version of Preferred Partner for the top 10% of Preferred partners.
- ~8% extra commission on top of standard (~23% total)
- +60% visibility boost above standard Preferred
- Invite-only from Booking.com

---

## Traveller Review Awards

Booking.com's annual recognition for properties with consistently high review scores (typically 8.0+ on their 10-point scale). Winners receive a badge displayed on their listing — no cost, pure recognition. Improves trust signals and conversion.

---

## Strategy Guidance

- **New listing**: Focus on content score and competitive pricing before joining programs
- **Soft season / low occupancy**: Genius discounts can fill gaps more cost-effectively than leaving nights empty
- **High-demand property**: Consider Preferred only if ranking gains produce bookings that justify the commission increase
- **Run both**: Genius and Preferred can stack — some properties do both for maximum exposure
