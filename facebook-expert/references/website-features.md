## News Feed

The Feed is the scrollable stream of posts from friends, Pages, and Groups you follow.

**Effective use:**
- Use **Feed Filters** (Friends, Groups, Pages) to surface specific content without unfollowing
- Pin "Favorites" (up to 30 friends/Pages) — their posts appear in a dedicated Favorites tab and higher in feed
- Click the three-dot menu on any post → **"Snooze for 30 days"** to temporarily hide a person/Page without unfollowing
- Use **"See First"** on Pages/friends you never want to miss (Page → Follow Settings → See First)
- The feed algorithm prioritizes: meaningful interactions → video watch time → post recency → relationship strength

**Feed ranking signals (2026):**
- Comments and shares outweigh reactions
- Original video content and Reels get preferential boost
- Content from close friends and frequent interactions ranks higher
- Clickbait and engagement-bait demoted

## Profile & Timeline

Your profile is your public-facing identity on Facebook.

**Sections:**
- **Intro** — job, education, location, relationship status, bio (500 chars)
- **Featured** — pin up to 5 photos/videos to the top of profile
- **About** — detailed info (places lived, contact, family, life events)
- **Friends** — visible friend list with mutual friends highlighted
- **Photos/Videos/Reels** — media organized by type

**Effective use:**
- Set audience on each About section individually (public, friends, only me)
- Use **Life Events** (under "About" or "Create Post → Life Event") to add milestone stories
- Enable **Profile Picture Guard** to prevent screenshots and shares of your profile photo
- Use **Professional Mode** (for Creators) to access follower analytics, Reels bonuses, and monetization without creating a Page

**Audience controls per post:**
- Public | Friends | Friends except… | Specific Friends | Only Me | Custom

## Stories

Ephemeral posts visible for 24 hours; appear at the top of the app.

**Types:** Photo, video (up to 20 sec), boomerang, text card, poll, question sticker

**Effective use:**
- Stories reach a different audience than feed posts — use them for time-sensitive announcements
- Add a **Link sticker** to any story to drive traffic (all accounts can use this, no follower requirement)
- Use **Poll** and **Question** stickers to drive engagement
- Archive stories automatically (Settings → Stories → Save to Archive) — accessible only to you
- Stories from Pages appear in followers' story tray; Stories from personal profiles appear for friends
- Cross-post from Instagram to Facebook Stories in one tap (if accounts are linked)

## Reels

Short-form video (up to 90 seconds) — Facebook's highest-reach format in 2026.

**Creation tips:**
- Use the Reels editor: add music, text overlays, stickers, green screen, or remix another Reel
- Optimal length: 15–30 seconds for maximum completion rate
- Add captions (auto-generated) — watch time increases ~12% with captions on
- Use trending audio for algorithmic boost
- All videos uploaded to Facebook are now Reels by default

**Distribution:**
- Reels appear in the dedicated Reels tab, in Feed, and on Watch
- Public Reels can reach non-followers (unlike regular posts)
- Cross-post to Instagram Reels simultaneously (linked accounts)
- Facebook watches 140 billion Reels/day globally (2026)

**Creator features:**
- **Content Protection**: Alerts when someone posts a matching Reel
- **Reels Bonuses**: Performance-based payouts for eligible creators (by invitation)
- **Multi-language audio**: AI-translated voiceovers in 7+ languages

## Facebook Live

Broadcast real-time video to your audience.

**Starting a Live:**
1. Tap the Live button in Create Post or open the Camera → Live tab
2. Add title, description, and choose audience
3. Select camera (front/back) and enable mic/camera permissions
4. Optional: Add guests (up to 4 people in a Live Room)

**Features during broadcast:**
- Live Q&A (pin viewer questions to screen)
- Polls during stream
- Stars (paid virtual currency from viewers — monetization)
- Live shopping (tag products during stream)
- Schedule a Live up to 7 days in advance to build anticipation

**Post-broadcast:**
- Live replay automatically posted to your profile/Page
- Clip segments into Reels (must do within 30 days)
- Download the replay video

**Best practices:**
- Go live at consistent times to build a regular audience
- Minimum viable live: 10+ minutes for algorithmic boost
- Engage comments in real time — responses boost reach

## Events

Create and manage events for online or in-person gatherings.

**Creating an event:**
1. Tap Events → Create Event
2. Choose: Public (anyone can see/join) or Private (invite-only)
3. Add: title, date/time, location or online link, cover photo, description
4. Co-hosts: add other Pages or people as co-hosts

**Features:**
- **Ticketing**: Integrate with Eventbrite or use Facebook's native paid events
- **Discussion tab**: Post updates and Q&A for attendees
- **Live video**: Broadcast directly from the event page
- **Recurring events**: Set weekly/monthly repeats
- **Event insights** (Pages only): attendance, reach, post engagement

**Discovery:**
- Events appear in the Events tab based on location, interests, friends attending
- Boost events as ads for paid reach

## Marketplace

Peer-to-peer buying/selling platform; also includes vehicle listings, rentals, and retail stores.

**Listing an item:**
1. Tap Marketplace → + Sell
2. Choose category (items, vehicles, homes for rent, jobs)
3. Add photos (up to 10) — AI auto-enhances lighting
4. Set price; AI suggests similar listings for pricing guidance
5. Choose delivery: local pickup, or shipping (with Facebook Checkout)

**Buyer features:**
- Visual similarity search — find visually similar items
- Filter by: category, price range, condition, location radius
- Offer counter-price directly in Messenger
- Saved searches with notifications for new matching listings

**Seller features:**
- Boost a listing as a paid Marketplace ad for more reach
- Manage offers and mark as sold in Seller Dashboard
- Shipping labels generated automatically (US domestic)
- Ratings build trust profile

**Safety tips:**
- Meet in public, well-lit locations for local pickup
- Use Facebook Checkout for payment protection (avoids direct cash/Venmo)
- Check buyer/seller ratings and profile history
- Report suspicious listings via the flag icon

## Facebook Dating

Optional opt-in feature (separate from main Facebook profile).

**Setup:**
- Facebook Settings → Dating → Get Started
- Creates a separate Dating profile — not visible to your Facebook friends
- Choose: orientation, gender, age range, distance preferences

**Features:**
- **Secret Crush**: Add up to 9 Facebook friends or Instagram follows — both get notified only if mutual
- **Events & Groups dating**: Match with people attending same Events or in same Groups
- **Video calling**: Built-in video dates before meeting in person

**Privacy:**
- Dating profile is separate from regular profile
- Friends and friends-of-friends are excluded from matches by default
- Blocked people on Facebook are also blocked on Dating

## Gaming

Casual gaming platform integrated into Facebook.

**Accessing games:**
- Facebook Gaming tab (left sidebar or bottom nav on mobile)
- Instant Games: play directly in browser/app without download

**Features:**
- Gaming groups: join communities around specific games
- Watch live gameplay streams from creators
- Tournaments: participate in or host gaming tournaments
- Gaming Profile: track your scores, achievements, and friends' activity

**Note (2026):** Facebook ended its Gaming Creator Program (partner support ended Oct 2025). Casual gaming features remain active, but dedicated streaming career features have moved to other platforms.

## Watch (Video)

Dedicated video hub featuring long-form content, shows, and live video.

**Content types:**
- Short clips from Pages and friends
- Long-form videos and series from creators/publishers
- Facebook Live replays
- Facebook Reels

**Effective use:**
- Follow specific creators via Watch for a dedicated video feed
- Use "Save Video" to watch later
- Create a Watch Party to watch videos simultaneously with friends (with live chat)
- Videos autoplay muted in feed; click for sound
