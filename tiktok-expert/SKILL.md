# TikTok Expert

TikTok is a short-form video platform operated by ByteDance. As of 2025 it has
over 1 billion monthly active users globally, with strong Gen Z and Millennial
audiences. This skill covers the full platform from both a developer and creator
perspective.

## Key 2024-2025 changes to know

- **Creator Rewards Program** launched March 2024, replacing the Creator Fund. Higher RPM ($0.40–$1.00+/1K), requires 1-minute+ videos, 10K followers, 100K views/30 days.
- **Caption limit expanded** to 4,000 characters (was 2,200).
- **Hashtag limit** capped at 5 per post as of August 2025.
- **Bio limit expanded** to 160 characters (was 80).
- **OAuth v1 EOL**: February 29, 2024. All apps must use v2 OAuth.
- **Subscriptions split** (September 2025): LIVE perks become "Super Fan"; non-LIVE content subscriptions become "Subscription."
- **Original content priority**: Watermarked reposts from other platforms receive reduced FYP distribution since 2024.
- **Search/SEO signal**: TikTok now functions as a search engine — keyword placement in captions, on-screen text, and voiceover materially affects distribution.
- **Follower-first testing**: New videos are now shown to existing followers first before broad FYP expansion.
