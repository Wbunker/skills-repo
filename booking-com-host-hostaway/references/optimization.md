# Booking.com Ranking & Optimization

## How Booking.com Search Ranking Works

Booking.com's algorithm weights:

1. **Content Score** — listing completeness and photo quality
2. **Availability** — how far out and how many dates are open
3. **Competitive pricing** — rate vs. comparable properties in your market
4. **Conversion rate** — how often your listing converts views to bookings
5. **Review score** — guests rate on a 10-point scale; higher = better ranking
6. **Response time** — faster responses improve ranking
7. **Host-initiated cancellation rate** — cancellations drop ranking significantly
8. **Program participation** — Genius and Preferred Partner both boost visibility

---

## Content Score: Target 90%+

Access in Extranet → Property → Property page score.

Key levers:
- **Photos**: 10+ high-quality images, 4K preferred. You cannot choose the cover photo — Booking.com selects it
- **Amenities**: list everything; every unchecked amenity is a missed filter result
- **Description**: complete and accurate; Booking.com auto-generates one — customize it
- **Policies**: cancellation, house rules, check-in times fully defined

---

## Pricing Strategy

- Use Hostaway dynamic pricing integrations (PriceLabs, Wheelhouse, Beyond) — syncs to Booking.com automatically
- Apply a **channel-specific markup** in Hostaway Listing Mapping to offset Booking.com's ~15% commission
- Flexible cancellation policies rank higher and convert better — factor this into pricing
- **Genius discount**: 10–20% off for loyalty travelers; only participate if occupancy needs filling
- **Promotions**: Booking.com Extranet → Promotions → offers like early bird, last-minute, or length-of-stay discounts

---

## Review Strategy

Booking.com uses a 10-point scale. Reviews display prominently and directly influence ranking.

- Respond to every review — public responses visible to future guests
- For negative reviews: calm, professional responses demonstrate accountability
- Booking.com sends review requests automatically post-stay
- Target 8.0+ (strong) → 9.0+ (exceptional); Traveller Review Award eligibility starts around 8.0
- Accurate listings prevent expectation gaps that drive low scores

---

## Availability & Calendar

- Keep calendar open **12+ months** out — longer availability window = more search visibility
- Never leave calendar stale — Booking.com ranks properties with active, updated calendars higher
- Trigger manual syncs from Hostaway after any pricing changes to ensure Booking.com reflects them quickly

---

## Response Time

Booking.com tracks how quickly you respond to guest messages. Faster responses = better ranking.

Use Hostaway automated messages to:
- Send immediate booking confirmations
- Answer common pre-arrival questions automatically
- Trigger pre-check-in instructions at the right time
