# Medium Partner Program

## Eligibility

To join the Partner Program:
- Must be a **Medium paying member** ($5/month or $50/year)
- Must have at least **1 published story** on Medium
- Must have a **Stripe account** connected (payment processor)
- Available in most countries; some restrictions apply

Enrollment: Settings → Partner Program → Enroll

---

## How Earnings Are Calculated (2026)

Earnings come from a proportional share of Medium's monthly membership revenue pool, distributed based on:

### 1. Member Reading Time (Primary Signal)
- How long **paying Medium members** spend reading your story
- Target: stories that hold attention for 3+ minutes earn significantly more
- Free readers do not count toward earnings
- Reading completion rate matters — readers who finish earn you more than those who bounce

### 2. Engagement Signals
- **Claps** from members
- **Highlights** from members
- **Replies/responses** from members
- Each boosts your earnings share from the pool

### 3. Boost Bonus
- Stories selected for Boost receive additional distribution AND a bonus earnings multiplier
- Boosted stories consistently outperform in earnings due to volume of member eyeballs

### 4. External Traffic Bonus (added Oct 2025)
- **5% bonus** on member reads that arrive from outside Medium (social media, newsletters, search, etc.)
- Incentivizes writers to share stories off-platform
- Applies to any external link click that leads to a member read

### 5. New Member Conversion Reward (added Feb 2026)
- When a **non-member** hits your paywalled story and becomes a Medium member to read it, you receive a **one-time bonus**
- Bonus = a small share of that new member's first membership payment
- Members who cancel within 1 month are excluded (anti-clickbait filter)
- Conversion tracking visible on partner dashboard (per-story reporting coming)
- After initial conversion, that member's future reads flow into the general pool

## Paywalling Stories

- You choose whether each story is free or members-only (paywalled)
- Paywalled stories show a teaser to non-members who can read the full version after subscribing
- **Friend Links**: share a unique URL that lets non-members read a paywalled story for free — does not count as a conversion but builds goodwill
- Only paywalled stories generate Partner Program earnings from member reads

## Earnings Reality

Rough ranges (varies enormously by niche, quality, and audience):
- Beginners (1–5 stories/month): $20–$200/month
- Intermediate (10+ optimized stories): $500–$2K/month
- Advanced (large audience, 50K+ views): $5K+/month

Top earners focus on: high-read-time topics, Boost eligibility, and driving external traffic.

---

## Strategy for Maximizing Earnings

1. **Write for read completion** — structure stories so members finish them
2. **Target Boost-eligible content** — expert perspective, research-backed, non-clickbait
3. **Drive external traffic** — share on social, newsletters; the 5% bonus adds up
4. **Paywall strategically** — paywall your best content; free content builds discovery
5. **Use Friend Links sparingly** — good for seeding shares without cannibalizing conversions
6. **Convert non-members** — clear, honest titles attract non-members who convert to paid
