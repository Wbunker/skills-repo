# Boost & Distribution

## Three Distribution Tiers

Medium distributes stories at three levels:

| Tier | What it means | How to get it |
|---|---|---|
| **Boost** | Wide distribution to all Medium readers; algorithmic promotion | Nominated by publication editors or Medium curators |
| **General** | Shown to your followers + tag followers + some discovery surfaces | Meeting Medium's distribution guidelines |
| **Network** | Only shown to your followers | Story doesn't meet general distribution guidelines |

Most stories land in General or Network. Boost is selective and high-impact.

---

## The Boost Program

Boost dramatically amplifies a story's reach — typically **500 to 100,000 extra views** within the first week, guaranteed minimum of 500 additional views.

Boost does not depend on your follower count. It's purely about content quality.

### How Stories Get Boosted

**Two pathways:**

1. **Boost Nomination Program** — Publication editors in the program nominate stories; Medium's curation team validates and approves
2. **Direct curation** — Medium's internal curators discover and Boost stories independently

---

## Boost Criteria (What Curators Look For)

Medium's curators assess stories on:

**Expertise & Perspective**
- Writer has genuine subject matter expertise OR relevant lived experience
- Story offers a new angle, re-examines something familiar, or sheds new light on a topic
- Not just a summary of what's already widely known

**Research & Credibility**
- Claims are backed by sources (links provided)
- Thorough rather than surface-level treatment

**Title & Subtitle Quality**
- NOT sensationalistic, clickbait, or tabloid-style
- NOT overly generic, mysterious, or formulaic
- Gives readers a clear, honest idea of what they'll get
- Curiosity gap is fine; deception is not

**Writing Quality**
- Well-edited, readable prose
- Appropriate structure (headers, paragraphs, pacing)
- Images/cover photo are relevant and non-stock where possible

**What Disqualifies a Story**
- Listicles without substance or insight
- Recycled conventional wisdom without new angle
- Misleading titles (also triggers anti-conversion filter in Partner Program)
- Thin content under ~4 minutes read time

## Boost Nomination Program (Publications)

Publication editors can join Medium's Boost Nomination Program and nominate their best stories for Boost consideration.

**Nominator requirements:**
- Submit at least 1 nomination per month
- Maintain nomination approval rate above **20%**
- Adhere to Medium Trust & Safety guidelines

**For writers:** Getting published in a Boost Nomination Program publication dramatically increases your chances of being Boosted. Medium maintains a list of 100+ participating publications in the Help Center.

---

## Distribution Guidelines (General Distribution)

To reach General distribution (minimum bar):
- Story must be original (not previously published elsewhere without canonical URL)
- Must follow Medium's Rules
- Paywall status doesn't affect distribution tier
- Canonical URL set correctly if cross-posting

Stories that fall to Network distribution: spam, low-effort, policy violations, or thin content.

---

## Algorithm Signals (2025–2026)

Medium's algorithm weighs:
- **Reading completion** — did members finish the story? This is the strongest ranking signal
- **Where readers stop** — the algorithm tracks scroll depth; drop-offs early = negative signal
- **Engagement rate** — claps, highlights, responses relative to views
- **Follower growth** — stories that earn new followers signal quality

Follower count is less important than it once was — a story from an unknown writer can outperform an established one if the content quality is higher.
