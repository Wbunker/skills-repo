## Which Plan to Choose

**Actively job searching → Premium Career**
- Applicant insights (how you rank vs. others)
- Salary data for specific roles
- 90-day profile viewer history (free = 5 days)
- "Under 10 Applicants" and "Actively Hiring" filters
- 5 InMail credits/month to contact hiring managers

**Building a personal brand or running a small business → Premium Business**
- Unlimited people browsing (free limits profile views after ~5/day)
- Company insights (employee growth, headcount trends)
- 15 InMail credits/month
- More powerful for outreach and research than Career plan

**B2B sales or business development → Sales Navigator Core (minimum)**
- 50+ lead filters, Buyer Intent signals, job change alerts
- Core plan is the entry point; Advanced adds AI tools (Account IQ, Message Assist)
- See [sales-navigator.md](sales-navigator.md) for full feature details

**Recruiting 1–5 hires/year solo → Recruiter Lite**
- 20+ filters, 30 InMail/month, basic pipeline tools
- Only covers 1st/2nd/3rd degree network (not full LinkedIn)

**High-volume enterprise recruiting → LinkedIn Recruiter**
- Full 900M+ member access, 40+ filters, 150 InMail/month, ATS integrations, team collaboration

**Not sure if Premium is worth it?** LinkedIn typically offers a free 1-month trial. Use it during an active job search or a sales push and evaluate ROI before committing.

## Free vs. Premium Key Differences

| Feature | Free | Premium Career | Premium Business |
|---|---|---|---|
| Profile viewers | 5 days | 90 days | 90 days |
| Applicant insights | No | Yes | No |
| Salary insights | Limited | Yes | Yes |
| People browsing | Limited (~5/day) | Expanded | Unlimited |
| InMail | 0 | 5/month | 15/month |
| Company insights | Basic | Basic | Full |
| LinkedIn Learning | No | Yes | Yes |
| Open to Work (recruiter-only) | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Who viewed your profile | 5 most recent | Full 90-day list | Full 90-day list |

**LinkedIn Learning:** Included with Premium Career and Premium Business. Also available standalone (~$39.99/month). 22,000+ courses; certificates display on your profile automatically.
