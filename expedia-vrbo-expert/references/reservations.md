# Reservations

## Viewing Reservations

**Location:** Reservations section of the dashboard

The reservation list shows:
- Upcoming arrivals
- Current occupancy
- Booking trends
- Full confirmed booking details (guest name, dates, unit, rate)

Filter by date range, booking status, or unit to narrow results.

---

## Modifying a Reservation

Reservations can be modified directly in the dashboard:
- Change check-in or check-out dates
- Adjust the nightly rate for the booking
- Update unit assignment (if applicable)

For hosts connected via a Property Management System (IPM), modifications should be made in the PMS — changes sync back to the dashboard automatically.

---

## Canceling a Reservation

### Guest-Requested Cancellation
Process on behalf of a guest:
1. Open the reservation in the Reservations section
2. Select **Cancel Reservation**
3. Choose whether to apply the cancellation policy (refund per policy) or issue a custom refund amount

If a cancellation fee applies per the cancellation policy, it is charged to the **Expedia Virtual Card** associated with the booking on the cancellation date.

**Custom refunds:** Hosts can manually refund amounts beyond (more generous than) what the standard policy requires.

### Reconciliation & No-Shows
- **No-show:** Mark a guest as a no-show through the reservation detail page
- **Reconciliation:** Confirm actual stay occurred vs. booked stay for billing accuracy

---

## Partner-Initiated Cancellations (Host Cancels)

Canceling a confirmed guest booking as the host carries penalties under Expedia Group's **External Partner-Initiated Cancellation Policy** (full policy at expediagroup.com/who-we-are/marketplace-policies/).

Penalties may include:
- Financial charges
- Reduced search ranking
- Listing suspension for repeated violations

**Never cancel a guest booking without reviewing the policy first.**

---

## Expedia Virtual Card

Expedia issues a virtual credit card per booking. This card is charged:
- For the booking value on the payout schedule
- For applicable cancellation fees on the cancellation date

The card number is accessible within the booking detail in the dashboard.
