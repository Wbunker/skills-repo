## Key Non-Obvious Facts

- **External links suppress reach** ~30% — move links to the first comment, not the post body.
- **Comments are 15x more valuable** than likes to the algorithm. Seed genuine comments in the first 90 minutes after posting.
- **LinkedIn Groups bypass InMail** — group members can message each other directly for free without being connected.
- **Connection requests cap** at ~100/week; quality targeting beats volume.
- **API access is tiered** — most useful API products (posting, analytics, ads) require Partner Program approval, not just a developer account.
- **Profiles cap at 30,000 first-degree connections**; after that, followers only.
