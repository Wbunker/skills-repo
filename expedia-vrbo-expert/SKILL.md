# Expedia / Vrbo Cabin Management Expert

## Portals

| Portal | URL | Best For |
|--------|-----|----------|
| **Vrbo Host Dashboard** | vrbo.com (sign in) | Primary interface for vacation rentals/cabins |
| **Expedia Partner Central (EPC)** | apps.expediapartnercentral.com | Hotel-style extranet; also valid for cabins |
| **Partner Resources Hub** | partner.expediagroup.com | Help docs, blog, training |

Cabins listed on Vrbo appear across the full Expedia Group marketplace (Expedia.com, Hotels.com, Orbitz, Travelocity, etc.) automatically.

## Listing Plans

- **Pay-Per-Booking:** Commission 10–30% per booking (default for most hosts)
- **Professional Plan:** Annual subscription; better for high-volume managers (10+ units)

## Gotchas

- Amenities must be explicitly listed or the cabin is excluded from filtered searches — missing amenities are invisible to guests filtering by those features.
- The Content Score sub-scores (amenities, room amenities, policies, photos) each affect search ranking independently — a high overall score with a weak photos sub-score still hurts.
- Partner-initiated cancellations carry financial penalties — never cancel a guest booking without understanding the External Partner-Initiated Cancellation Policy first.
- Cancellation deadlines are 11:59 p.m. in the **property's time zone**, not the guest's.
- iCal sync has a delay (typically 15–30 min) — it does not prevent double bookings in real time; a channel manager is more reliable for multi-platform hosts.
- The Accelerator tool is pay-per-stay (charged only on completed stays), not pay-per-click — it carries no upfront cost risk.
- Listing minimum requirements must all be met before a listing can go Active; a single missing element keeps it in "Incomplete" status.
