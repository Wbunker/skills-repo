## Account Privacy

### Private vs. Public Account
**Path:** Profile → Menu (☰) → Settings and Privacy → Account Privacy → Private Account

| Setting | Public | Private |
|---|---|---|
| Who sees posts | Anyone | Approved followers only |
| Who can follow | Anyone (instant) | Must be approved |
| Appears in Explore | Yes | No |
| API access to profile | Yes (public data) | Limited |
| Suitable for | Brands, creators, businesses | Personal accounts |

**Note:** Switching to Private does not affect existing followers — only new follower requests require approval.

### Who Can See Your Content
- **Posts**: All followers (public) or approved followers only (private)
- **Stories**: All followers, Close Friends only, or hide from specific people
- **Reels**: Public by default even on private accounts (can restrict in settings)
- **Live**: All followers (can set to Practice Mode for private rehearsal)

### Profile Information Visibility
Settings → Privacy → Profile:
- **Pronouns**: visible to followers or everyone
- **Connections**: hide/show mutual followers

## Stories & Close Friends Privacy

### Close Friends
A curated list of accounts that see exclusive green-ring Stories and content.

**Managing:**
- Profile → Menu → Close Friends
- Add or remove accounts at any time
- People on your list are **not notified** they're on it
- People removed are **not notified** either
- You can now remove yourself from someone else's Close Friends list (2026)

**Close Friends content:**
- Stories with green ring (Close Friends only)
- Subscriber-only Stories (for Creator accounts with Subscriptions)
- Highlights created from Close Friends Stories are only visible to Close Friends

### Hiding Stories from Specific People
- Create a Story → tap "Audience" → "Hide Story From" → select accounts
- Those people still follow you but won't see that specific Story

### Story Reply Controls
Settings → Privacy → Messages and Story Replies → Story Replies:
- Everyone
- People you follow
- No one (disable replies)

## Interaction Controls

### Comment Controls
Settings → Privacy → Comments:
- **Who can comment**: Everyone / People you follow / People you follow and your followers / No one
- **Filter offensive comments**: Toggle on to auto-hide comments with common offensive words
- **Manual filters**: Add custom words/phrases to hide; applies to all posts
- **Restrict comments**: Block comments from specific accounts without them knowing

### Tagging & Mentions
Settings → Privacy → Tags and Mentions:
- **Allow tags from**: Everyone / People you follow / No one
- **Manually approve tags**: Review before tags appear on your profile
- **Allow mentions from**: Everyone / People you follow / No one

### Activity Status
Settings → Privacy → Activity Status:
- Turn off to hide when you were last active in DMs
- When off, you also lose the ability to see others' activity status

### Restricted Accounts
See [Blocking, Restricting & Muting](#blocking-restricting--muting).

## Account Security

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
**Path:** Settings → Accounts Center → Password and Security → Two-Factor Authentication

**Methods:**
1. **Authenticator App** (Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password) — most secure
2. **WhatsApp** — OTP sent via WhatsApp message
3. **SMS** — OTP sent via text (least secure; vulnerable to SIM swap)
4. **Security Key** (FIDO2 hardware key) — highest security

**Recommendation:** Use an authenticator app. Never use SMS if your account has significant following or monetization.

### Login Activity
Settings → Accounts Center → Password and Security → Where You're Logged In:
- View all active sessions (device, location, time)
- Tap any session → Log Out to revoke access
- Review regularly; remove any unrecognized devices

### Password
- Settings → Accounts Center → Password and Security → Change Password
- Use a unique password stored in a password manager
- Instagram will notify you if your email appears in known data breaches

### Passkeys (2026)
Instagram supports passkeys as an alternative to passwords:
- Settings → Accounts Center → Password and Security → Passkeys
- Uses Face ID, Touch ID, or device PIN
- Phishing-resistant — recommended for all users

### Suspicious Login Alerts
- Instagram sends email + in-app alert when a login is detected from a new device/location
- If you receive an alert you didn't trigger: immediately change password and enable 2FA

### Account Recovery
If locked out:
1. Login screen → "Forgot password?" → enter username/email/phone
2. If account is hacked and email changed: check original email for "Your email was changed" message — Instagram provides a link to reverse it
3. Use "Need More Help?" to submit identity verification

## Data Controls

### Download Your Data
Settings → Your Activity → Download Your Information:
- Exports all posts, Stories, messages, followers, following, comments, search history, and more
- Delivered as a ZIP file to your email within a few days

### Off-Instagram Activity
Settings → Your Activity → Off-Instagram Activity:
- See which apps and websites send your browsing data to Instagram
- Clear history: disconnects past activity from your ad profile
- Manage Future Activity: toggle off to stop future data being used for ad targeting

### Ad Preferences
Settings → Ads:
- View and edit interest categories
- See which advertisers have uploaded your contact info
- Disable personalized ads based on data from partners

### Account Deletion vs. Deactivation
- **Deactivate**: Profile temporarily hidden; can reactivate anytime by logging back in
  - Settings → Accounts Center → Personal Details → Deactivate Account
- **Delete**: Permanent; 30-day grace period before full deletion
  - Settings → Accounts Center → Personal Details → Delete Account

## Blocking, Restricting & Muting

### Block
- Completely prevents a person from seeing your profile, posts, Stories
- They cannot message you or find your account in search
- They are not notified
- You are also removed from their follower/following lists
- **Path:** Tap their profile → ··· → Block

### Restrict
A softer moderation tool for handling harassment without escalating.

- Their comments on your posts are **only visible to them** (others can't see it)
- Their DMs go to your Message Requests (you don't get a notification)
- They cannot see your read receipts or active status
- They are **not notified** that you've restricted them
- **Path:** Swipe left on a comment or DM → Restrict; or Profile → ··· → Restrict

**When to use Restrict vs. Block:**
- Use **Restrict** for: potential harassers you don't want to escalate, people you want to monitor quietly
- Use **Block** for: severe harassment, stalkers, spam accounts, anyone you want zero contact with

### Mute
- Stop seeing someone's posts/Stories in your feed without unfollowing
- They are not notified
- **Path:** Tap ··· on a post → Mute (mute posts, mute stories, or both)

### Hide
- On Stories: hide a specific person's Stories from appearing in your feed
- Tap and hold their Story circle → Hide

### Summary Table

| Action | They see you | You see them | They're notified | Followers removed |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Block** | No | No | No | Yes |
| **Restrict** | Yes | Yes | No | No |
| **Mute** | Yes | No (in feed) | No | No |
| **Unfollow** | Yes | No | No | One-way |
