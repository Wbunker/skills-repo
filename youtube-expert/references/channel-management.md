## Channel setup checklist

Access channel customization at **studio.youtube.com → Customization**. Three tabs: **Layout**, **Branding**, **Basic Info**. All changes must be **Published** to go live.

- [ ] Upload profile picture (800×800 px)
- [ ] Upload banner image (2560×1440 px; safe zone 1546×423 px)
- [ ] Set handle (@username)
- [ ] Write channel description
- [ ] Add website and social links (up to 14; 5 overlay on banner)
- [ ] Add business contact email
- [ ] Set channel trailer (for non-subscribers)
- [ ] Set featured video (for returning subscribers)
- [ ] Create featured sections (home page layout)
- [ ] Upload watermark (optional)

## Branding tab

### Profile picture
- **Size:** 800×800 px recommended; displayed as a circle
- Appears everywhere: comments, search results, video watch pages, sidebar
- Should be a face or recognizable logo — human faces perform better in comments
- Use the same image across all platforms for brand consistency

### Banner image
- **Total canvas:** 2560×1440 px
- **Safe zone:** 1546×423 px centered — text and logos MUST be within this zone
  - Safe zone is visible on all devices; outer areas are cropped on mobile and TV
  - Left/right edges visible only on wide desktop monitors; full banner visible on TV
- **File type:** JPG, PNG, BMP, GIF (no animation)
- **Max file size:** 6 MB
- Include: channel name, tagline, upload schedule, social handles

### Video watermark
- Appears as a small overlay on all videos (bottom-right corner by default)
- Display options:
  - **End of video** — appears in last 15 seconds only
  - **Custom start time** — appears from specified timestamp to end
  - **Entire video** — appears throughout
- Clicking the watermark prompts subscription
- Recommended size: 150×150 px, PNG with transparent background

## Basic Info tab

### Channel name
- What appears in search results and on your channel page
- Should be easy to pronounce, spell, and remember ("tell a friend" test)
- Can differ from your handle (@username)

### Handle
- Unique @username across YouTube (e.g., @ChannelName)
- Used in: direct links (youtube.com/@handle), @mentions in comments, channel tags
- Should match your username on other social platforms when possible
- Minimum 3 characters; only letters, numbers, hyphens, underscores, periods

### Description
Appears on the About tab of your channel page. Structure:
```
[What the channel covers — clear value proposition]
[Who it's for — target audience]
[Why you're credible — experience, credentials, perspective]
[Upload schedule or content cadence]
[CTA — subscribe, link, newsletter]
```
Max 1,000 characters. Keywords here contribute to channel-level search indexing.

### Links
- Up to 14 links total
- First 5 links appear as overlays on the channel banner (on desktop)
- Prioritize: website, newsletter, top social platforms
- Link text is customizable (e.g., "Free Newsletter" instead of raw URL)

### Business contact email
- Separate from your Google account email
- Shown publicly on About tab for brand/sponsorship inquiries
- Use a business or dedicated contact email, not personal

## Layout tab

### Channel trailer
Plays automatically for **non-subscribers** who visit the channel page. See [Channel trailer vs featured video](#channel-trailer-vs-featured-video).

### Featured video
Plays for **returning subscribers** instead of the trailer. See [Channel trailer vs featured video](#channel-trailer-vs-featured-video).

### Featured sections
Up to 12 sections can be arranged on the channel homepage. See [Featured sections](#featured-sections).

## Handles and URLs

| URL type | Format | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Handle URL | `youtube.com/@handle` | Primary URL; shareable and memorable |
| Channel ID URL | `youtube.com/channel/UCxxxxx` | Permanent; never changes |
| Legacy custom URL | `youtube.com/c/name` | Old format; still works but use handle URL |

The handle URL is the recommended URL to share. It redirects correctly even if you change your channel name.

## Channel trailer vs featured video

| | Channel trailer | Featured video |
|--|----------------|----------------|
| Shown to | **Non-subscribers** visiting channel page | **Returning subscribers** visiting channel page |
| Goal | Convert visitors to subscribers | Re-engage existing subscribers |
| Recommended length | Under 45 seconds | Any length; choose best recent upload |
| Content focus | Who you are, what the channel offers, why subscribe now | Best or most recent video; series entry point |
| Auto-play | Yes, muted on page load | Yes, muted on page load |

**Trailer best practices:**
- First 5 seconds must hook — assume no context
- Clearly state who the channel is for ("If you're a small business owner who wants to...")
- Include one explicit CTA at the end: subscribe
- Don't use a regular upload as your trailer — create a dedicated 30–45 second pitch

## Featured sections

Featured sections organize your channel homepage. Up to **12 sections** can be active at once. Reorder by dragging.

### Section types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Latest uploads | Auto-populates with most recent public videos |
| Popular uploads | Auto-populates with highest-view videos |
| Short videos | Auto-populates with your Shorts |
| Live streams | Past and upcoming live streams |
| Single playlist | Displays one playlist you choose |
| Multiple playlists | Shows several playlists as a row (up to 12 playlists per row) |
| Featured channels | Feature other channels (good for collaborators or sister channels) |
| Upcoming live streams | Shows scheduled broadcasts |
| Completed live streams | Past live stream archive |
| Memberships | Displays membership perks (requires YPP) |

**Recommended homepage order:**
1. Featured video / trailer (set in Layout tab, not sections)
2. Latest uploads (so returning visitors see what's new)
3. Popular uploads (social proof for new visitors)
4. Core series playlists (2–4 playlists organized by topic)
5. Shorts (if applicable)

## Community posts

Community posts appear in subscribers' feeds and on the Community tab. Available when channel has 500+ subscribers.

**Post types:**
- Text (up to 5,000 characters)
- Image (max 1600×1600 px; aspect ratio 2:5 to 5:2; up to 5 images)
- Video (link to a YouTube video)
- Poll (2–5 options)
- Quiz (multiple choice with a correct answer)

**Use cases:**
- Announce new uploads between posting cadence
- Share behind-the-scenes content
- Run polls to gauge audience interest in future topics
- Repurpose short-form content from other platforms
- Drive engagement signals (likes, comments) between video uploads

**Community posts appear in:**
- Subscriber home feeds
- The channel's Community tab
- YouTube notifications (for notified subscribers)
