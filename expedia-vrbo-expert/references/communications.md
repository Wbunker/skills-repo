# Guest Communications

## Messages Tool

**Location:** Messages section of the dashboard

### Features
- **Smart templates** — pre-written messages for common scenarios (booking confirmation, check-in instructions, check-out reminder)
- **Scheduled messages** — automatically sent at a defined time before/after check-in or check-out
- **Filters** — organize conversations by booking status, date, or property
- **Host-initiated messaging** — reach out to guests before they message first

### Scheduling Check-In Instructions
Attach check-in instructions directly to the listing (shown to guests at booking) or schedule them to send automatically a set number of days before arrival. Include:
- Gate/door codes
- Parking instructions
- Check-in time and process
- Emergency contact

---

## In-House Feedback Tool

Expedia sends an email to guests on their **arrival day** prompting real-time feedback on:
- Check-in experience
- The cabin
- Location

This gives hosts a window to address issues proactively before checkout — preventing a negative review.

**Action:** Monitor the Messages section on arrival days for any feedback responses that signal a problem.

---

## Guest Reviews

### Receiving Reviews
Post-stay reviews appear in the dashboard. Hosts are notified via the dashboard and mobile app.

Reviews are consolidated across Expedia Group platforms (Expedia.com, Vrbo, Hotels.com, etc.) — one review pool.

### Responding to Reviews
Respond directly through the dashboard or mobile app. Best practice:
- Respond to all reviews (positive and negative)
- For negative reviews: acknowledge the issue, explain what was corrected
- Keep responses professional — they are public

**Review score impact:** A 4.6+ review score is required to qualify for Premier Host status. See [performance.md](performance.md).

---

## Mobile App

The Vrbo/Expedia partner mobile app provides:
- **Real-time notifications** for new bookings, cancellations, and reviews
- **Faster response times** — app users respond 20% faster than non-app users (response rate affects Premier Host score)
- **Camera with Photo Score feedback** — upload photos directly with quality guidance
- Full message management

Recommend installing the app to maintain response rate requirements for Premier Host status.

## Inquiry Management (IPM / Integrated Partners)

If connected via a Property Management System:
- **New Messaging API** integration: respond directly from within the PMS
- **Inquiries Integration** only: view inquiries in the dashboard, respond via host dashboard

Check connectivity type in Settings or via the connectivity provider.
