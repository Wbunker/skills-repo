# Klydoclock — Content, Klydos, and Features

## What Is a Klydo?

A Klydo is a short, looping animation created by an independent artist. Each Klydo is designed to be displayed as the moving "face" of the analog clock — the clock hands overlay the animation at all times.

- Animations are typically 5–30 second loops
- They play at 60 FPS on the dual circular HD displays
- New Klydos are added to the library regularly by the Klydo community
- Artist profiles: https://www.klydoclock.com/pages/klydo-artists

## Content Library

The active Klydo library contains approximately **400–500 animations** as of 2025–2026. The vendor has marketed a goal of 1500+ Klydos; the difference represents works still in production or queued for future release.

Content is delivered over WiFi when connected. Without internet, the clock displays whichever Klydos it has already downloaded.

## Daily Klydo

Included with every Klydoclock purchase:
- One new featured Klydo is highlighted each day
- Downloads automatically when the clock is online

## Collections

Klydos are grouped into **Collections** — curated themed sets (e.g., nature, abstract, seasonal, holiday). Browse collections via:

**☰** → Browse → Collections

Collections help find related artwork without scrolling the full library.

## Favorites

Heart any Klydo to save it to your personal favorites list:

- Press the **♥ center button** while a Klydo is displayed
- Heart icon appears on-screen to confirm
- Access favorites: **☰** → Browse → Favorites
- Un-heart: press ♥ again on the same Klydo

## Content Cycling

The clock can automatically rotate through Klydos on a schedule. Configure the interval:

**☰** → Settings → Display → Cycle Frequency

| Setting | Behavior |
|---------|---------|
| 1 min | New Klydo every minute |
| 5 min | Every 5 minutes |
| 15 min | Every 15 minutes |
| 60 min | Hourly |
| 6 hours | 4 times per day |
| 24 hours | Once per day (curated daily Klydo) |
| Fixed | Never auto-changes; navigate manually with ← → |

## Manual Navigation

At any time, press **← →** on the remote to jump to the previous/next Klydo regardless of the cycle frequency setting.

## Subscription Model

The Klydoclock is a one-time purchase (no ongoing subscription fee as of 2025). The daily Klydo and full library access are included with device ownership.

Custom or branded Klydo creation is available as a separate paid service: https://www.klydoclock.com/pages/custom

## What the Clock Does Not Do

- No alarm function
- No calendar or date display (time only)
- No weather, notifications, or smart home integration
- No app control (app is "coming soon" per vendor)
- No user-uploadable custom animations (except via the custom Klydo service)
