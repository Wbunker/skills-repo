## Feed

The main scrollable stream of posts from accounts you follow.

**Feed types:**
- **Home** (default): Algorithmic mix of followed accounts + recommendations
- **Following** (tab): Chronological posts from accounts you follow only
- **Favorites** (tab): Posts from up to 50 accounts you've marked as Favorites — shown first

**Feed ranking signals:**
- Relationship strength (interactions, DMs, tags, searches)
- Interest prediction based on past behavior
- Post recency and popularity
- Time spent on similar content

**Post formats in Feed:**
- Photo (single or multiple — swipeable carousel)
- Video (up to 60 min; shorter videos auto-become Reels)
- Reels (short video, shown in feed and Reels tab)
- Text posts (limited rollout)

**Post audience controls:**
- All followers (default)
- Close Friends only (Stories only — feed posts are always public to followers)
- Collaborator posts: co-author with another account (appears on both profiles)

**Cross-posting:**
- Share feed posts to Stories (tap airplane icon → Add post to your story)
- Instagram → Threads cross-posting: global test underway for image posts (not Reels yet, 2026)
- Instagram → Facebook cross-posting: enable in Settings → Linked Accounts

## Stories

Ephemeral content visible for 24 hours; appears in circles at the top of the app.

**Creating a Story:**
- Tap your profile circle or the + icon → Story
- Formats: Photo, Video (up to 60 sec), Boomerang, Superzoom, Layout (collage), Text

**Interactive stickers:**
- Poll, Quiz, Slider, Questions, Countdown, Link, Music, Location, Hashtag, Mention, Chat
- **Animated Collages (2026)**: Select 5–20 photos → auto-combined into animated Story clip
- **AI Transitions (2026)**: Multiple photos combined with AI-powered transitions between them

**Audiences:**
- All followers (default)
- **Close Friends** (green ring) — custom list of people who see exclusive Stories
- **Instagram Plus** (testing 2026): paid subscription tier adding multiple Story audiences, rewatch insights, viewer-list search, longer Story expiration, super hearts, and a Spotlight option

**Story music:**
- Add licensed music via the music sticker
- AI-suggested music based on your listening preferences (2026 test)

**Notes on Stories:**
- You can now add one Note per post or Reel shared to Stories, visible to mutual followers or Close Friends

**After posting:**
- See who viewed your Story (viewers list, up to 24 hours after posting)
- Viewers can react with emojis or reply via DM
- Archive Stories automatically: Settings → Archive → Story Archive

## Reels

Short-form vertical video — Instagram's highest-reach format.

**Length:** Up to 3 minutes (expanded from 90 seconds in 2026)
**Aspect ratio:** 9:16 vertical (1080×1920px recommended)

**Creation tools:**
- **Teleprompter (2026)**: Built-in teleprompter directly in the Reels creation menu
- Audio: add music, voiceover, or original audio
- Effects: AR filters, green screen, transitions, speed control
- Text overlays, stickers, captions (auto-generated)
- Templates: remix existing Reels with the same structure
- Dual camera: record front and back simultaneously

**Playback:**
- **Tap to pause** (2026): Single tap to pause a Reel instantly

**Distribution:**
- Reels tab (dedicated vertical scroll feed)
- Home feed (mixed with other content)
- Explore page
- Reels reach non-followers — best format for organic growth

**Cross-posting:**
- Share Reels to Facebook simultaneously (Settings → Linked Accounts → Share Reels to Facebook)
- Save drafts before publishing

**Performance tips:**
- Hook in first 1–2 seconds
- Add captions — watch time increases with text on screen
- Use trending audio for algorithmic boost
- Optimal length for completion rate: 15–30 seconds
- Consistent posting: 3–5 Reels/week for growth

## Instagram Live

Real-time video broadcast to your followers.

**Starting a Live:**
1. Swipe right (camera) → select **Live** at the bottom
2. Add a title, set audience (everyone / Practice Mode / close friends)
3. Schedule a Live up to 90 days in advance

**During Live:**
- Pin a comment to keep Q&A visible
- Add a guest (Live Rooms: up to 3 guests total)
- Enable/disable comments
- Share a link in the chat
- Use **Practice Mode** to go live privately before broadcasting

**Viewer engagement:**
- **Badges** (monetization): viewers buy $0.99/$1.99/$4.99 heart badges
- Questions: viewers can submit questions; pin them to answer
- Reactions: emoji flood the screen

**After Live:**
- Save the replay to your camera roll or share to IGTV/Feed
- Replays available for 30 days (by default)

## Direct Messages (DMs)

Private messaging between accounts.

**Message types:**
- Text, photos, videos, voice messages, GIFs, stickers
- Ephemeral media: "View Once" photos/videos that disappear after viewing
- Share posts, Reels, and Stories directly via DM
- **Blend** (2026): Create a shared Reels feed in a 1:1 or group DM — both people's Reels recommendations merged into one feed

**Message Requests:**
- Messages from accounts you don't follow go to Requests
- Accepting = they can message freely; declining removes without notification

**Group chats:**
- Up to 250 members
- Add name, photo, and role admins
- Polls in group DMs

**Connected to Facebook Messenger:**
- If linked, Facebook friends can message you via Instagram
- Manage this in Settings → Messages and Story Replies

## Explore

Discovery feed surfacing content from accounts you don't follow.

**Content types:**
- Photos, Reels, videos; organized by topic channels (Beauty, Sports, Travel, etc.)
- Reels have their own tab within Explore

**Search:**
- Search accounts, hashtags, places, and audio
- Visual search: tap the camera icon to search by image

**Algorithmically driven by:**
- Past interactions (saves, likes, shares, profile visits)
- Interest categories derived from your activity

**Getting onto Explore:**
- Consistent use of relevant hashtags
- Strong save and share rates on posts
- Reels with high completion rate
- Content tagged with location

## Notes

Short, ephemeral text updates (up to 60 characters) that appear at the top of your followers' DM inbox.

**How to use:**
- DMs tab → tap your profile photo at the top → Leave a note
- Expires after 24 hours
- Visible to: mutual followers or Close Friends
- Replies go to your DMs

**2026 addition:** You can now attach one Note to a post or Reel when sharing it to Stories.

---

## Blend

Shared Reels discovery feed between two (or more) people, accessed in DMs.

- In a DM conversation → tap the Blend icon
- Creates a combined feed of Reels recommendations based on both users' interests
- Designed for sharing and discovering Reels together in real time
- Available in 1:1 DMs and group DMs

---

## Friends Map

Snap Map-style real-time location sharing feature.

- Opt-in only; location sharing is voluntary
- See where friends are and what they're posting from those locations
- Control visibility: specific friends only or all followers
- Turn off: Settings → Privacy → Location → Friends Map

---

## Highlights

Permanent collections of past Stories displayed on your profile.

- Create from archived Stories (even after they've expired)
- Choose a custom cover image and name
- Highlights from Close Friends Stories are **only visible to your Close Friends list**
- Edit highlights anytime: add/remove individual Stories
- No limit on number of highlights

**Best practice:** Use Highlights as a persistent "about us" or FAQ section for business/creator profiles.
